MEGA MILLIONS® JACKPOT GROWS TO $330 MILLION FOR JANUARY 4 DRAWING
(Austin) ­ The Texas Lottery® announced that the Mega Millions® estimated jackpot for the drawing to be held on Tuesday, January 4, has increased from $290 million to an estimated $330 million. "Due to extraordinary sales for the New Year's Eve Mega Millions drawing, we are able to increase the jackpot to a much higher level," said Texas Lottery Executive Director Gary Grief. "This is exciting for us and especially exciting for our players, who now have an opportunity to win an estimated $330 million for just a $1 play." Mega Millions is sold by 42 lotteries across the country. The highest lottery jackpot in history was a Mega Millions jackpot of $390 million in March 2007. Texas has had eight Mega Millions jackpot winners since joining the game in December 2003. For more information about the Texas Lottery and Mega Millions visit www.txlottery.org. ###
