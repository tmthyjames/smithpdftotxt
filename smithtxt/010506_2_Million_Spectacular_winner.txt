ALICE PLAYER CLAIMS TOP PRIZE IN $2,000,000 SPECTACULAR SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ Julia A. McIlhany of Alice yesterday claimed the second of three $2 million top prizes in the Texas Lottery® $2,000,000 Spectacular scratchoff game. "I feel so fortunate to be in such a `spectacular' position," said McIlhany. "What a great way to start the new year!" McIlhany purchased her $20 $2,000,000 Spectacular ticket at the Adrian's Drive-In Grocery located at 601 South Reynolds Street in Alice. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. "$2,000,000 Spectacular was definitely spectacular for Mrs. McIlhany," said Robert Tirloni, products manager for the Texas Lottery Commission. "And as of today, one more top prize for this game remains unclaimed, along with thousands of other spectacular cash prizes in amounts ranging from $20 to $20,000!"
