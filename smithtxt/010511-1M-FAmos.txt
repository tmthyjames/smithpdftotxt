SAN ANTONIO RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION PRIZE IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(Austin) ­ The 26th prize of $1 million in the $500,000,000 Blockbuster game was claimed by Fredric R Amos of San Antonio. With more than half-a-billion dollars in total prizes, the $500,000,000 Blockbuster features a top prize of $2.5 million. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. For selling the winning ticket, the H-E-B Food Store #108 located at 20935 U.S. Highway 281 North in San Antonio is eligible to receive a $10,000 retailer bonus. Amos requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $34 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $12 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $12 million.
