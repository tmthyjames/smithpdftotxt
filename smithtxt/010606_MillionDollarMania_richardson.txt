SECOND MILLION DOLLAR MANIA TOP PRIZE CLAIMED BY RICHARDSON RESIDENT
(AUSTIN) ­ Rizwan G. Bhatti of Richardson today claimed the second of three $1 million top prizes in the Texas Lottery® Million Dollar Mania scratchoff game. "Mr. Bhatti's Million Dollar Mania top prize is the second one claimed this week and yesterday we had a $2 million top prize claimed in $2,000,000 Spectacular," said Robert Tirloni, products manager for the Texas Lottery Commission. "That's $4 million in top prizes claimed in just three days, so 2006 is off to a great start for our players. But, as always, we want to remind everyone to play responsibly!" Bhatti purchased his ticket at the Mr. H Food Store located at 2300 Rowlett Rd. in Rowlett. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. The winner requested minimal publicity. In addition to the $1 million top prizes, the $20 Million Dollar Mania game offers cash prizes in amounts ranging from $20 to $10,000. Tonight's Mega MillionsTM jackpot is worth an estimated $15 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $44 million.
