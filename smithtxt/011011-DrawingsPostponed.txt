DALLAS COWBOYS AND HOUSTON TEXANS
DRAWINGS POSTPONED DUE TO WEATHER

(Austin) ­ The Dallas Cowboys and Houston Texans second-chance drawings originally scheduled for Tuesday, January 11, and Wednesday, January 12, have been rescheduled due to inclement weather. The Dallas Cowboys drawing will be Thursday, January 13, and the Houston Texans drawing will be Friday, January 14. For more information about these games or the Texas Lottery®, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
