TEXAS LOTTERY® PLAYER FROM PEARSALL WINS BIG ON $200 MILLION CASH SPECTACULAR TICKET
(AUSTIN) ­ Felix Palacios of Pearsall claimed the sixth of 10 top prizes of $1 million each in the $200 Million Cash Spectacular scratchoff game. In addition to the four top prizes, four second-tier prizes of $100,000 and millions of prizes of $10 to $10,000 currently remain unclaimed in the game. Overall odds of winning any $200 Million Cash Spectacular prize are one in 3.56, including break-even prizes. For selling the winning ticket, the Brothers Self Serve located at 1104 W. Comal St. in Pearsall is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $20 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $39 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $15 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs, including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance. The Texas Lottery provides several entertaining games for lottery players including Powerball®, Mega Millions®, Lotto Texas®, All or NothingTM, Texas Two Step®, Pick 3TM, Daily 4TM, Cash Five® and scratch-offs.
