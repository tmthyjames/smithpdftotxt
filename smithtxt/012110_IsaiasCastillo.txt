MAN FROM GARLAND WINS TOP PRIZE OF $10,000 PER MONTH IN MONTHLY BONUS FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(Austin) ­ Isaias Castillo of Garland has collected the top prize in Monthly Bonus, $10,000 per month for 20 years. The game is a Texas Lottery® scratch-off ticket.

This is the last of the four top prizes of $10,000 per month for 20 years offered in this game to be claimed. With no top prizes left, game closing procedures will begin.

Castillo bought the ticket at the One Star Food MT & CK Cashing, 510 Plano Road, in Garland. The store is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket.

The new winner requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $105 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $36 million.

For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Web site at http://www.txlottery.org. ###
