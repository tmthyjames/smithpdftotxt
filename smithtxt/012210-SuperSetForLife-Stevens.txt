DALLAS RESIDENT WINS TOP PRIZE OF $7.5 MILLION FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY® IN SUPER SET FOR LIFE
(Austin) ­ Mark Steven Roberts of Dallas today claimed the top prize in Super Set For Life, which gives him $500,000 per year for 15 years. The prize totals $7.5 million.

Roberts bought the ticket at Mac's Corner, 1098 E. Northwest Highway, in Grapevine. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this top-prize-winning ticket.

This is the fourth and last top prize in this game to be claimed, which initiates gameclosing procedures.

The new winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $105 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $36 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, please visit the official Web site at http://www.txlottery.org. ###
