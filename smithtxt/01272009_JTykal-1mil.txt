NAVASOTA MAN JOINS TEXAS $50 MILLION CLUB
(AUSTIN) ­ The Texas $50 Million Club instant game from the Texas Lottery® minted a new millionaire yesterday when Navasota resident Joseph G. Tykal claimed the third of five $1 million top prizes. Featuring a total of more than $51.4 million in prizes, the Texas $50 Million Club offers overall odds of one in 2.88, including break-even prizes. For selling the winning ticket, the Shell Express Mart located at 9323 Highway 20 South in Navasota is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $30 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $13 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Web site at http://www.txlottery.org.
