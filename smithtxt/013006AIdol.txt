DIDN'T MAKE IT TO THE TOP 12 ON AMERICAN IDOL? YOU CAN STILL WIN A TOP PRIZE FOR THE TEXAS LOTTERY® AMERICAN IDOL SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ You won't get a recording deal out of it, however you could land a sweet deal of a different kind with the latest Texas Lottery® scratch-off game, AMERICAN IDOL. Players can find the pop-culture craze at any authorized Texas Lottery retailer starting Wednesday, February 1. "With more than $12 million in cash prizes available to win in the AMERICAN IDOL scratch game, we hope to hear about many Texas Lottery players `singing' all the way to the bank," said Anthony J. Sadberry, Acting Executive Director for the Texas Lottery Commission. There are 13 top cash prizes of $20,000 for players to win. Other cash prizes for the AMERICAN IDOL ticket range from $2 to $2,000. The overall odds of winning any prize, including break-even prizes, are 1 in 4.31. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www.txlottery.org
