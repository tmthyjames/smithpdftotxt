$2.4 MILLION SCRATCH-OFF PRIZE CLAIMED LEAVING ONE TOP PRIZE UNCLAIMED IN MONTHLY

BONUS GAME

(AUSTIN) ­Lonnie Gooden III of Austin claimed the third of four Monthly Bonus top prizes worth $10,000 per month for 20 years, a total of $2.4 million. Only one top prize remains unclaimed in the Texas Lottery® Monthly Bonus scratch-off game. Gooden said simply, "I'm a believer now." With more than $52 million in total prizes, the Monthly Bonus features overall odds of one in 4.94, including break-even prizes. Nine second-tier prizes, worth $20,000 each, remain unclaimed, along with many other prizes ranging from $5 to $2,000.
