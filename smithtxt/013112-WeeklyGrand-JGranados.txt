HOUSTON RESIDENT CLAIMS TOP PRIZE IN WEEKLY GRAND SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ Jorge Alberto Granados of Houston yesterday claimed the top prize--$1,000 a week for 20 years--in the Texas Lottery® Weekly Grand (Game #1355) scratch-off game. The total amount of the prize before taxes is $1,040,000. This is the second of four top prizes offered in this game to be claimed.

The winning ticket was purchased at Fiesta Mart #8, located at 2323 Wirt Road in Houston. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket.

The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $170 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $18 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $23 million.

For more information about the Texas Lottery, visit www.txlottery.org.
