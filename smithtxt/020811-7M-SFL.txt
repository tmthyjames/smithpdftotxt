SUPER SET FOR LIFE II AWARDS TOP PRIZE OF $7.5 MILLION TO CENTRAL TEXAS TRUST
(Austin) ­ The SFL Revocable Trust, by Regina M. Stump, Trustee, of Woodway, claimed a Super Set For Life II top prize of $500,000 per year for 15 years. Trust members presented the winning ticket at the Texas Lottery® headquarters in Austin today. With more than $106 million in total prizes, Super Set For Life II features overall odds of winning any prize of one in 3.08, including break-even prizes. This is the second of four top prizes to be claimed in the game. Two $7.5 million top prizes and 21 second-tier prizes of $10,000 remain unclaimed. For selling the winning ticket, the CEFCO Food Store #83, 8306 N. Highway 6 in Waco, is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $19 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $63 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $22 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org.
