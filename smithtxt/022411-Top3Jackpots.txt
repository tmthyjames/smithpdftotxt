TEXAS LOTTERY® PLAYERS CAN TRY FOR TOP THREE U.S. JACKPOTS THIS WEEKEND
(Austin) ­ Friday and Saturday will be exciting days for fans of Texas Lottery® games. Three of the drawings this weekend will feature the largest grand/jackpot prizes in the country. The rankings for the annuitized prizes are shown below. 1. The estimated Powerball® jackpot is $184 million. 2. The estimated Mega Millions® jackpot is $76 million. 3. The estimated Lotto Texas® jackpot is $27 million. "Texas is the place for lottery fans this weekend," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Gary Grief. "We are excited to offer our customers the three highest jackpots available in the United States in three of our most popular games." Players can participate in the Mega Millions game Friday night, followed by Powerball and Lotto Texas on Saturday. The Texas Lottery reminds players to sign the back of each ticket and play responsibly. It only takes one ticket to win. Odds of winning and how-to-play information can be found on the official Texas Lottery website at www.txlottery.org. ###
