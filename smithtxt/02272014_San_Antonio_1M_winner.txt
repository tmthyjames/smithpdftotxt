SAN ANTONIO SCRATCH-OFF PLAYER WINS $1,000,000 JACKPOT PRIZE WORTH $1 MILLION

(AUSTIN) ­ Gloria A. Rodriguez of San Antonio claimed a $1 million top prize in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game $1,000,000 Jackpot. Rodriguez purchased her ticket at the Fuel Station #3, 3202 West Ave. in San Antonio, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

The $1,000,000 Jackpot game features more than $90 million in total cash prizes and overall odds of one in 2.88, including break-even prizes. One top prize of $1 million and four second-tier prizes of $10,000 currently remain unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimum publicity.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $42 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $16 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
