WILSON FAMILY LIVING TRUST CLAIMS WEEKLY GRAND TOP PRIZE FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(Austin) ­ The Wilson Family Living Trust, Jeff Wilson, trustee, of Plano today claimed the $1,040,000 top prize in the popular Weekly Grand scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery®. Wilson purchased the ticket at Parker Beverages, 3300 Coit Road, in Plano. For selling this ticket, the retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000. Today's claim takes the third of the game's four top prizes. The first Weekly Grand game debuted in 1995. The trustee requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $20 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $59 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $200 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Web site at http://www.txlottery.org.
