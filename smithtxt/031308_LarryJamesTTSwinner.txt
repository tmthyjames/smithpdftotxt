$1,350,000 TEXAS TWO STEP® JACKPOT CLAIMED BY FORT WORTH RESIDENT
(AUSTIN) ­Larry James of Fort Worth has claimed the $1,350,000 Texas Two Step® jackpot for the drawing on February 28. James purchased the ticket at the Cash & Carry, 2401 Ascension Boulevard, Suite 100, in Arlington. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus that equals one percent of the jackpot prize, capped at $10,000. The numbers were 1 - 7 - 23 - 33, and the bonus ball was 11. James selected the numbers on the ticket. The winner requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $59 million.
