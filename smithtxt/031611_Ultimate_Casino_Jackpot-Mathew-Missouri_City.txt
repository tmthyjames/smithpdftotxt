  MISSOURI CITY RESIDENT WINS $7.5 MILLION IN ULTIMATE CASINO JACKPOT SCRATCH-OFF
(Austin) ­ Sam Mathew of Missouri City claimed a top prize of $7.5 million in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off, Ultimate Casino Jackpot. Sam's FastStop FoodMart, 2020 Sam Houston Avenue, in Huntsville sold the ticket. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket. Ultimate Casino Jackpot was issued in March of 2010. This is the second of this $50 game's three top prizes of $7.5 million that has been claimed. The odds of winning any prize in Ultimate Casino Jackpot, including break-even prizes, are one in 2.46. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $5 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $67 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $201 million. More information about the Texas Lottery is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
