HOUSTON RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION PRIZE PLAYING $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER SCRATCH-OFF
(Austin) ­ Camille M Duncan of Houston today claimed a prize of $1 million, the second-tier prize in $500,000,000 Blockbuster, a scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery®. She bought the ticket at the Randall's #1011, 14610 Memorial Drive, in Houston. For selling this ticket, the retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000. Duncan claimed the 16th of the 40 prizes of $1 million the game offers. Six of the game's top prizes of $2.5 million remain unclaimed. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $20 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $62 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $28 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Web site at http://www.txlottery.org.
