JAMES E. SMITH OF SWEETWATER CLAIMS $1 MILLION IN $1 MILLION CASH BLAST FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­ James E. Smith of Sweetwater has claimed the top prize in $1 Million Cash Blast from the Texas Lottery®. Smith bought the ticket on Saturday at Taylor Petroleum, located at 1101 E. Broadway in Sweetwater. The store is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for having sold the ticket. $1 Million Cash Blast was released on March 5. This is the first of three top prizes to be claimed in this game. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $37 million.
