ALEDO RESIDENT WINS MEGA MILLIONS® SECOND-TIER PRIZE OF $1 MILLION
(Austin) ­ James Berry of Aledo won a Mega Millions® second-tier prize that totaled $1 million in the March 15 Mega Millions drawing. The second-tier prize is worth $250,000, but, because Berry purchased the Megaplier® option, the second-tier prize became a guaranteed $1 million. The numbers drawn that night were 10 - 11 - 12 - 28 - 43, and the Mega Ball was 45. Berry bought his Quick Pick ticket at the Bearcat Shamrock, 421 Highway 1187 S, in Aledo. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $7 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $101 million. Friday Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $304 million. More information about the Texas Lottery® is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
