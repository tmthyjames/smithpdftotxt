MONTHLY BONUS TOP PRIZE OF $10,000 PER MONTH FOR 20 YEARS GOES TO HOUSTON RESIDENT
(Austin) ­ Louis E. Armstrong of Houston won the Monthly Bonus scratch-off game's top prize of $10,000 per month for 20 years. Armstrong purchased the ticket at the Fiesta Mart #5, 5600 Mykawa, in Houston. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket. Monthly Bonus remains a popular Texas Lottery® scratch-off game. First issued in 2005, it has been re-issued six times since then. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $7 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $101 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $304 million. More information about the Texas Lottery is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
