PLATINUM PRESENT GAME TO CLOSE SOON,
MCKINNEY RESIDENT CLAIMS LAST TOP PRIZE
(Austin) ­ The Platinum Present scratch-off game will close April 1, having paid out all three of its $1 million top prizes. Larry Collins of McKinney claimed the final top prize at the Texas Lottery® Claim Center in Dallas. Collins, who requested minimal publicity, purchased his ticket at the 1 Stop Food Store, 103 N. Graves Street in McKinney. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible to receive a $10,000 retailer bonus. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $12 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $153 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $9 million. To learn more about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official website, www.txlottery.org.
