SULLIVAN CITY RESIDENT CLAIMS TOP PRIZE IN TOP PRIZE $500,000 FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­ Miguel A. Ramirez of Sullivan City in South Texas has claimed the top prize in Top Prize $500,000 from the Texas Lottery®. Ramirez purchased the ticket at the Benavides Drive In, Southside of Highway 83, in Sullivan City. This is the third of five top prizes to be claimed in Top Prize $500,000. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $74 million.
