AUSTIN MAN CLAIMS TOP PRIZE IN MONTHLY BONUS FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­ Salvador O. Guerrero of Austin has claimed the top prize of $10,000 per month for 20 years in Monthly Bonus, a Texas Lottery® scratch-off game. The prize does not exceed $2.4 million. He purchased the ticket at Muchos Imports #2, 1723 E. Oltorf, in Austin. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the ticket. This is the first of the three top prizes in this game, #839, to be claimed. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $12 million.
