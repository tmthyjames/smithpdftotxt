TEXAS LOTTERY® BLACK SERIES II ­ LIMITED EDITION SCRATCH-OFF AWARDS FIRST TOP PRIZE OF $1 MILLION

(Austin) ­ The first of six $1 million top prizes in the Texas Lottery® Black Series II ­ Limited Edition scratch-off game was claimed with a ticket purchased at My Store #1, 10810 Ferguson Road in Dallas, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. Introduced March 14, the Texas Lottery® Black Series II ­ Limited Edition game features more than $44 million in prizes. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 3.32, including break-even prizes. Odds to win the top prize are one in one million. Texas Lottery® player Farouq M. Said of Garland claimed the $1 million prize and requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $30 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $218 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $11 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org.
