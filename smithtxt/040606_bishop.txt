BISHOP WOMAN MAKES TRIP TO LOTTERY HEADQUARTERS TO COLLECT TOP PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Joan Ginther of Bishop today collected $2,000,000, the top cash prize offered for the Texas Lottery® Holiday Millionaire scratch game. "It's always our pleasure to greet top-prize winners at lottery headquarters," said Acting Executive Director, Anthony J. Sadberry. "I would like to congratulate Ms. Ginther on her prize." Ginther purchased her Holiday Millionaire scratch-off ticket at Big Diamond #106 at 525 South 14th in Kingsville. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the prize-winning ticket. This is the second of three $2,000,000 top prizes in the Holiday Millionaire game to be claimed. Other cash prizes for the $30 ticket range from $30 to $20,000. Overall odds of winning, including break-even prizes, are 1 in 2.17. The winner has requested minimal publicity.
