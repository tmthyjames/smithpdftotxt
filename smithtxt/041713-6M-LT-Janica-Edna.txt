EDNA RESIDENT CLAIMS $6 MILLION

LOTTO TEXAS®JACKPOT
Lotto Texas with Extra!TM debuts tonight
(AUSTIN) ­ Lesa Janica of Edna claimed the $4,587,242.40 cash value of the $6 million advertised jackpot from the April 13 Lotto Texas® drawing today at Texas Lottery® headquarters in Austin. Janica's ticket was the only ticket sold to match all six numbers drawn, 4 ­ 13 ­ 14 ­ 18 ­ 26 ­ 38. "This is very exciting, but also a little overwhelming," said Janica. "We just feel so fortunate and blessed by this opportunity." Janica purchased her ticket at the Conner Corner Grocery-Edna located at 102 E. Houston Highway in Edna. The store is eligible for a $60,000 retailer bonus for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. "Congratulations to Ms. Janica; she's our sixth Lotto Texas jackpot winner already in 2013," said Texas Lottery Executive Director Gary Grief. "And with the exciting new changes coming to Lotto Texas starting with tonight's drawing, we're looking forward to meeting even more winners." About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $20 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $40 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $15 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs, including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance. The Texas Lottery provides several entertaining games for lottery players including Mega Millions®, Powerball®, Lotto Texas with Extra, All or NothingTM, Texas Two Step®, Pick 3TM, Daily 4TM, Cash Five® and scratch-offs.
