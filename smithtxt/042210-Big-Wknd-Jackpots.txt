BIG WEEKEND FOR TEXAS LOTTERY® PLAYERS: TOP JACKPOTS IN THE NATION UP FOR GRABS IN MEGA MILLIONS®, LOTTO TEXAS® AND POWERBALL®
(Austin) ­ Texas Lottery® players who are faithful fans of Lotto Texas® and also partial to trying their luck with Powerball® and Mega Millions® have the top three jackpots in the country to look forward to this weekend.

"With Mega Millions standing at an eye-catching $166 million, Lotto Texas rolling to a big $82 million, and Powerball at a significant $20 million, Texas Lottery players can choose from among the top three jackpots in the country," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Gary Grief. "These are the kinds of jackpots our players have told us they enjoy seeing, and while the big numbers generate big excitement, we always want to remind our players to play responsibly. It takes only one ticket to win."

For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official website at http://www.txlottery.org. ###
