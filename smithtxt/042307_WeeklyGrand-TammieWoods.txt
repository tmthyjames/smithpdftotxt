DALLAS WOMAN WINS TOP PRIZE OF $1,040,000 PLAYING WEEKLY GRAND FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­ Tammie Woods of Dallas has claimed the Weekly Grand top prize of $1,040,000, the equivalent of approximately $1,000 per week for the next 20 years. She purchased the ticket at the 7-Eleven #33120 that's located at 724 Hebron Parkway in Lewisville. The store is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket. This is the second of the game's three top prizes to be claimed. The game was issued in late November of 2006. Ms. Woods requested minimal publicity. Tuesday's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $47 million.
