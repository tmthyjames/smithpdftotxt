LEAGUE CITY PARTNERSHIP CLAIMS APRIL 7 LOTTO TEXAS® $75 MILLION JACKPOT
(AUSTIN) ­ Panjo Investments, Ltd, Tremarcor, LLC, General Partner, by John Stanford, Jr, president, of League City has claimed the $75 million Lotto Texas® jackpot for the drawing on April 7. The Cash Value Option amount of the prize is $45,014,921.84. Stanford purchased the ticket from the Quick Mart at 2100 Marina Bay Drive in League City. The store is eligible to receive $500,000, the maximum retailer bonus for selling this Lotto Texas ticket. Representatives of Panjo Investments, Ltd., requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas jackpot is worth an estimated $4 million.
