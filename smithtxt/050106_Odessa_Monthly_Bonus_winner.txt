ODESSA TRUST CLAIMS MONTHLY BONUS TOP PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ On Friday, Pedro Sanchez Jr., trustee of the PMS Family Trust of Odessa, claimed the top prize ­ $10,000 a month for 20 years ­ in the Texas Lottery® Monthly Bonus scratch-off game. The total amount of the prize before taxes is $2,400,000. "On behalf of the Texas Lottery Commission, I'd like to congratulate today's Monthly Bonus claimant," said Anthony J. Sadberry, acting executive director of the Texas Lottery Commission. The winning ticket was purchased at the Stockade Grocery & Deli, located at 807 North FM 1936 in Odessa. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. The winner requested minimal publicity. In addition to the top prizes, the $5 Monthly Bonus game offers cash prizes in amounts ranging from $5 to $20,000. Tuesday's Mega Millions jackpot is an estimated $41 million and Wednesday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $4 million.
