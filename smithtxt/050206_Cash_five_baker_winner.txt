$16,649 ­ THAT'S HOW THE COOKIE CRUMBLED FOR DALLAS CASH FIVE® WINNER

Leah D. Baker of Dallas claimed $16,649 from the April 28 Cash Five drawing.
(Austin, May 2) - Leah D. Baker of Dallas claimed $16,649 yesterday for holding one of two tickets sold that matched all five numbers in last Friday's Texas Lottery® Cash Five® drawing. A little after-dinner inspiration helped Baker choose what turned out to be the winning numbers: 4-17-31-33 and 37.

"I used the numbers from a fortune cookie," Baker explained. "When I checked the numbers in the paper the next day, I could hardly believe it. That was the best cookie I ever had!" Baker said she planned to share some of her winnings with her family, but her first purchase was a Lotto Texas® ticket. "We certainly want to congratulate Ms. Baker on her Cash Five good `fortune'," said Anthony J. Sadberry, acting executive director of the Texas Lottery Commission. "But of course, with (more)

Cash Five drawings are held Monday through Saturday at 10:12 p.m. Players win by matching two, three, four or five of five numbers drawn from a field of 37. The overall odds of winning a prize in Cash Five are 1 in 8. Baker purchased her winning ticket at the 7-Eleven located at 757 E. Beltline in DeSoto. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $41 million and Wednesday's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $4 million.
