HOUSTON RESIDENT WINS $50,000 PLAYING POWERBALL® IN MID-APRIL
(Austin) ­ Vincent A Taylor of Houston won $50,000 in the April 16 Powerball® drawing and collected his prize late Friday at the Texas Lottery® Claim Center in Houston. Taylor bought two Quick Pick Powerball tickets at Plaza Card & Gifts, 918 Louisiana #M150, in Houston. One of his tickets had four of the numbers drawn and the Power Ball number. Taylor also purchased the Power Play® option, which multiplied his prize of $10,000 by five, the Power Play number for that night's drawing. The numbers selected in the April 16 drawing were 21-33-44-45-55, and the Power Ball was seven. Taylor requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow night's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $51 million. The Lotto Texas® jackpot for Wednesday is an estimated $7 million, and the Powerball jackpot is an estimated $36 million. More information about the Texas Lottery can be found on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
