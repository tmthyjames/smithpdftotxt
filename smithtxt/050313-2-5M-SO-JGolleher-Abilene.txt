ABILENE RESIDENT CLAIMS $2.5 MILLION TOP PRIZE IN $500 MILLION FRENZY SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ Jerlene M. Golleher of Abilene claimed the sixth of 10 $2.5 million top prizes in the $500 Million Frenzy scratch-off game at Texas Lottery® headquarters on Friday. The $20 scratch-off game features more than $500 million in total prizes, including 10 prizes worth $2.5 million each and 40 prizes worth $1 million each. Overall odds of winning any $500Million Frenzy prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Golleher purchased her ticket at the 7-Eleven #72 located at 3102 Grape St. in Abilene. The retailer is eligible for a $10,000 bonus for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $40 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $15 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs, including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
