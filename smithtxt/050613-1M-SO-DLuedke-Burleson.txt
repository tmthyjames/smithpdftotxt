BURLESON RESIDENT WINS $1 MILLION FROM $500 MILLION FRENZY SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ David B Luedke, of Burleson, claimed a $500 Million Frenzy prize worth $1 million yesterday. This means 20 second-tier prizes of $1 million and four top prizes of $2.5 million are still waiting to be claimed. The $20 Texas Lottery® scratch-off game features more than $500 million in total prizes, including 10 prizes worth $2.5 million each and 40 prizes worth $1 million each. Overall odds of winning any $500Million Frenzy prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Luedke's winning ticket was purchased at the Do So Shell, 1200 SW Wilshire Blvd. in Burleson. The retailer is eligible for a $10,000 bonus for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $40 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $15 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs, including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
