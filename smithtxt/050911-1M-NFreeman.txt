$500 MILLION FRENZY GAME AWARDS FIFTH OF 40 $1 MILLION PRIZES TO PLAYER FROM AUSTIN
(Austin) ­ The Big Diamond #961, located at 9601 FM 1325 in Austin, is eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000 after selling a $500 Million Frenzy ticket worth $1 million to Nicholas Freeman of Austin. Freeman's prize is the fifth prize of $1 million to be claimed. All 10 top prizes of $2.5 million currently remain unclaimed, along with 35 second-tier prizes of $1 million. "The $500 Million Frenzy started with a bang, and it remains one of our best-selling scratch-offs," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Gary Grief. "With more than half-a-billion dollars in total prizes and 50 prizes of $1 million or more, it's no surprise that Texas Lottery players enjoy this game." Freeman requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $18 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $65 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $9 million.
