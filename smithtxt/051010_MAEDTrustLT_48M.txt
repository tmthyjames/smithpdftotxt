MAED TRUST OF HOUSTON COLLECTS $48.8 MILLION

LOTTO TEXAS® JACKPOT FROM OCTOBER 2009 DRAWING
(Austin) ­ MAED Trust, through Amegy Bank N. A., Trustee, by R. Sells Neuhaus, SVP, of Houston today collected the Lotto Texas® Cash Value Option jackpot prize of $48.8 million from the $76 million drawing on October 21, 2009. The Quick Pick ticket was purchased from a self-service terminal at the HEB Pantry Foods #288, 207 E South Street in Alvin. Because the ticket was sold at this retail location, the store has already received a retailer bonus of $500,000. This prize is an authorized bonus equal to one percent of the advertised jackpot and capped at $500,000. The numbers drawn were 9-20-42-51-53-27. In addition to the jackpot-prize-winning ticket, there were 35 other tickets that matched five of the six numbers drawn, with each of those winning a prize of $2,375. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $19 million. Wednesday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $89 million and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $97 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, please visit the official website at txlottery.org. ###
