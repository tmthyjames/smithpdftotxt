PLAYER FROM ARLINGTON CLAIMS FINAL TOP PRIZE OF $1 MILLION IN TEXAS LOTTERY® BLACK
(Austin) ­ Ruma Basu of Arlington claimed the last of six $1 million top prizes offered in the Texas Lottery® Black scratch-off game. With all of the top prizes claimed, the end of game date will be June 24. Players will have until December 21 to redeem their prizes. The Texas Lottery® introduced the follow-up game Texas Lottery® Black Series II Limited Edition in mid-March. Currently, two top prizes of $1 million remain unclaimed in the new game. Basu, who requested minimal publicity, purchased this ticket at Jackson's Grocery & Grill #1, 1823 S. Fielder Road in Arlington. For selling the winning ticket, the retailer is eligible for a bonus of $10,000. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $65 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $9 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $27 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, visit www.txlottery.org. ###
