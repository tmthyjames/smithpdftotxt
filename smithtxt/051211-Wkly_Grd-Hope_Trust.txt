THE HOPE TRUST OF COLLEGE STATION WINS $1,000 PER WEEK FOR 20 YEARS IN WEEKLY GRAND GAME
(Austin) ­ The Hope Trust, by Wesley Hall, Trustee, of College Station today collected the top prize of $1,000 per week for 20 years in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game, Weekly Grand.

Franky's Food Mart, 2801 S. Texas Ave. in College Station sold the ticket. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket.

This is the third of the four top prizes offered in this game to be claimed. The representative of the trust requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $27 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $10 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $81 million. More information about the Texas Lottery is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
