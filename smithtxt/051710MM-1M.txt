BIG LAKE RESIDENT WINS $1 MILLION IN MEGA MILLIONS®
(Austin) ­ Mickey Davidson of Big Lake has collected a Mega Millions® prize totaling $1 million from the Texas Lottery®.

Davidson's ticket matched the five numbers (20-21-40-47-56) drawn minus the Mega Ball (12) number in Friday night's drawing. The Megaplier number was four, which multiplied Davidson's prize of $250,000 into $1 million. He purchased the ticket at the Kent Kwik #431, 1105 E. 2nd Street in Big Lake.

In addition to Davidson, there were 32,001 more winners in Friday's Mega Millions drawing. They won prizes ranging from $2 to $10,000.

The winner requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions jackpot is an estimated $42 million. Wednesday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $93 million and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $145 million.
