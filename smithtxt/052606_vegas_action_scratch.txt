Trust Collects Top Prize For Texas Lottery® $3,000,000 Vegas Action Scratch-Off Game
(AUSTIN, May 26) - Tri Cong Lau, trustee of The Lau Family Trust of San Antonio, claimed the top cash prize for the Texas Lottery® $3,000,000 Vegas Action scratch game. The $30 ticket offers players the chance to win up to $3,000,000, paid out in a one-time lump-sum payment. "It's always our pleasure to greet top-prize winners at lottery headquarters," said Acting Executive Director Anthony J. Sadberry. "I would like to congratulate our latest winner and remind players there are still two $3,000,000 prizes left unclaimed for this game." The prize-winning scratch-off ticket was purchased at South Park News, located at 2310 S.W. Military Drive #229 in San Antonio. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the prize-winning ticket. This is the first of three $3,000,000 top prizes in the $3,000,000 Vegas Action game to be claimed. Other cash prizes for the $30 ticket range from $30 to $20,000. Overall odds of winning, including break-even prizes, are 1 in 2.11. The representative of The Lau Family Trust has requested minimal publicity.
