HOUSTON AREA PRODUCES TWO TEXAS LOTTERY® WINNERS IN DIFFERENT SCRATCH GAMES: PEARLAND WOMAN WINS $10,000 PER MONTH FOR 20 YEARS; KATY WOMAN WINS $1 MILLION
(AUSTIN) ­ Two Texas Lottery® scratch games have made two Houston-area women the latest winners of major prizes. Cynthia A. Wells of Pearland has collected the top prize of $10,000 per month for 20 years playing Monthly Bonus. Her prize will total $2.4 million after the final payment. Wells bought her ticket at the Broadway Diamond Shamrock, 7201 Broadway, in Pearland. The store is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for having sold this ticket. By playing $500,000,000 Blockbuster, Concepción O. Guerrero of Katy won $1 million. She collected it at the Lottery's Houston Claim Center. Guerrero purchased the ticket at the J & N Mini Mart #1, 22450 Franz Road, in Katy. This retailer also is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket. "Both winners had `firsts' for their respective tickets," said Texas Lottery Commission Deputy Executive Director Gary Grief. "In Monthly Bonus, this was the first of the game's four top prizes to be awarded. The other winner claimed the first of the 40 prizes of $1 million offered in $500,000,000 Blockbuster, a game that was issued just last week."
