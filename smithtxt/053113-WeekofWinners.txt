IT'S BEEN A WEEK OF WINNERS FOR THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­ It's been a week of winning for lottery players in Texas. On Wednesday, a Bells, Texas, retailer sold the state's first Powerball® jackpot-winning ticket, a $40 million prize. While the Powerball jackpot winner has not claimed the prize yet, several other lucky Texans are celebrating this week, including some new millionaires. From Bells to Arlington and Houston to Amarillo, big winners are popping up all over the Lone Star State.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $40 billion in prizes to lottery players.
