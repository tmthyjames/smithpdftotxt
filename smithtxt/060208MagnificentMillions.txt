SAN ANTONIO WOMAN WINS $1 MILLION PLAYING MAGNIFICENT MILLIONS FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­ Mai Ling Yee of San Antonio has won the top prize of $1 million in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game, Magnificent Millions. This is the first of the 10 prizes of $1 million offered by this game to be claimed. The $20-game went on sale last week. Yee, who requested minimal publicity, purchased the ticket from the Schertz Conoco, 1008 FM 78, in Schertz. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this topprize-winning ticket. Tuesday's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $25 million.
