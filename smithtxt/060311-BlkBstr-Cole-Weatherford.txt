WEATHERFORD RESIDENT WINS $1 MILLION PRIZE IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER SCRATCH-OFF GAME

(Austin) ­ Weatherford resident Jackie R. Cole claimed a second-tier prize of $1 million in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game, $500,000,000 Blockbuster. The ticket came from the Pic Up Mart, 102 W. Park Ave. in Weatherford. This prize of $1 million makes the retailer eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket. To date, 30 of the 40 second-tier $1 million prizes have been claimed. The game also offers 10 top prizes of $2.5 million, and only three of those remain unclaimed. The odds of winning any prize in $500,000,000 Blockbuster are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Cole requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $15 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $16 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million. More information about the Texas Lottery can be found on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
