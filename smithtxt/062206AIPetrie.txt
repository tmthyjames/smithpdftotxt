CALIFORNIA TRANSPLANT COLLECTS $20,000 FROM AMERICAN IDOL SCRATCH GAME

(AUSTIN) ­ Originally from California, T.J. Petrie, now of Leander, just won American Idol ­ the Texas Lottery® version, anyway. Petrie found she didn't have to stay in California to win a dream prize since she became the 10th recipient to claim $20,000, the top prize for the Texas Lottery American Idol scratch game. "This is the largest amount I've ever won in my life," said Petrie. "I bought three of the American Idol scratch games and didn't realize I had won anything at first." The American Idol scratch game, released earlier this year, allows players a chance to win up to 10 times. Cash prizes range from $2 up to $20,000. As of
