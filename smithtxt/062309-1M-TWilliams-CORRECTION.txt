-CORRECTIONAMARILLO RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION PRIZE WITH $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER INSTANT TICKET
(AUSTIN) ­ Amarillo resident Todd M. Williams claimed the third of 40 second-tier prizes worth $1 million in the $500,000,000 Blockbuster scratch-off game. Ten top prizes of $2.5 million each remain unclaimed. With more than half a billion dollars in total prizes, the $500,000,000 Blockbuster offers overall odds of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Williams purchased the ticket at the Toot `N Totum #67 at 4520 S. Georgia St.* in Amarillo, and the retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $68 million. Wednesday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $17 million.
