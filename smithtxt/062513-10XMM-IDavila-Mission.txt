TEXAS LOTTERY® SCRATCH-OFF PLAYER WINS $1 MILLION PRIZE PLAYING 10X MEGA MONEY
(AUSTIN) ­ Mission resident Israel A. Davila scratched his way to a $1 million top-prize in the Texas Lottery's® 10X Mega Money scratch-off game. "I thought my eyes were playing tricks on me," said Davila. "It was kind of a shock." Davila purchased his ticket at Fiesta's Mart located at 901 W. Business Hwy 83 in Mission. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. On playing Texas Lottery games, Davila said, "It's fun and enjoyable, just the relaxation of scratching a ticket." The $10 10X Mega Money game was introduced in January and features more than $200 million in total prizes. Five $1 million top prizes and five second-tier prizes of $100,000 currently remain unclaimed. Overall odds of winning any 10X Mega Money prize are one in 2.88, including break-even prizes.
