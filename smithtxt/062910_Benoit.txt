HOUSTON RESIDENT WINS $1 MILLION PLAYING

$500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER FROM TEXAS LOTTERY®
(Austin) ­Rodney J. Benoit of Houston has claimed a prize of $1 million, the second-tier award in $500,000,000 Blockbuster, a Texas Lottery® scratch-off ticket. Of the 40 second-tier prizes available in this game, Benoit's is the 19th claimed. $500,000,000 Blockbuster debuted in May of 2009. Since then, four of the 10 top prizes of $2.5 million have been awarded.

Benoit purchased the ticket at the Eagle Mart #3, 11997 Main Street, in Houston. The store is eligible to receive a retailer bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket.

The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $16 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $12 million, and Powerball® is an estimated $27 million.
