WEEKLY GRAND $1.04 MILLION
TOP PRIZE WON BY TYLER RESIDENT
(Austin) ­Habibur Rehman Khan of Tyler today claimed the top prize of $1,000 per week in the Texas Lottery's Weekly Grand scratch-off game.

The total prize at the end of 20 years is $1,040,000. The prize claimed today is the last of the four top prizes in this scratch-off. As a result, game-closing procedures will begin immediately.

Khan purchased the ticket at the Tyler Truck Stop, 801 South Southeast Loop 323, in Tyler. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket.

The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $14 million, and Powerball® is an estimated $54 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $43 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
