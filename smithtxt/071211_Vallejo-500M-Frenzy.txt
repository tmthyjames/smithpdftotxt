WOMAN FROM SAN ANTONIO WINS TOP PRIZE WORTH $2.5 MILLION IN $500 MILLION FRENZY SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(Austin) ­Blanche Vallejo of San Antonio claimed a prize worth $2.5 million today at Texas Lottery® headquarters in Austin. She won the top offering playing the popular scratch-off, $500 Million Frenzy. She bought the ticket at the Three Star Mart #1, located at 4301 Fredericksburg Road in Balcones Heights (San Antonio area). The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket. Vallejo's is the first of the game's 10 prizes of $2.5 million to be claimed. Issued in March of this year, $500 Million Frenzy also gives players the opportunity to win lower-tier prizes that range from $1 million, $25,000, and $10,000 down to amounts that include $1,000, $500, $100 and the break-even prize of $20. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $24 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $9 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $48 million. More information about the Texas Lottery is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
