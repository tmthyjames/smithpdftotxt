ONLY ONE TOP PRIZE REMAINS UNCLAIMED IN TEXAS LOTTERY BLACK-SERIES II-LIMITED EDITION
(Austin) ­ Luis Flores III of Seguin claimed the fifth top prize of $1 million in the Texas Lottery Black-Series IILimited Edition scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery®. One top prize and three second-tier prizes of $10,000 each currently remain unclaimed. Overall odds for winning any prize are one in 3.32, including break-even prizes. The odds of winning the top prize are one in one million. A new version of the game called Texas Lottery BlackSeries III-Limited Edition started on July 5, and all six top prizes remain unclaimed in that game. For selling the winning ticket, the Pic n Pac #03 located at 3051 Highway 123 South in Seguin is eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000. Flores requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $63 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $111 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $13 million.
