EIGHTH TOP PRIZE AWARDED IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(Austin) ­ Tommy Seals of Sherman today claimed the eighth of 10 top prizes worth $2.5 million each in the $500,000,000 Blockbuster scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery®. Ten second-tier prizes of $1 million also remain unclaimed. With more than a half a billion dollars in total prizes and 50 cash prizes of $1 million or more, odds of winning any prize in $500,000,000 Blockbuster are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. For selling the winning ticket, the Get & Go, located at 2001 S. Crockett in Sherman, is eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000. Seals requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $111 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $13 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $74 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, visit www.txlottery.org. ###
