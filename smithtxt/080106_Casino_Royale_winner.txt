GONZALES SCRATCH-OFF PLAYER CLAIMS $1 MILLION CASINO ROYALE TOP PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Carolyn S. Padilla of Gonzales yesterday claimed the first of two $2 million top prizes in the Texas Lottery® Casino Royale scratch-off game. Padilla purchased her $20 Casino Royale ticket at the Texas Tote located at 1315 Seydler in Gonzales. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. "We've looked forward to meeting our first Casino Royale top-prize winner since the game debuted in March, so we were extremely pleased to meet and congratulate Ms. Padilla today," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Anthony J. Sadberry. The winner requested minimal publicity. For more information about the Texas Lottery Commission, please visit the official Web site at http://www.txlottery.org. ...
