CALVERT TRUST COLLECTS LOTTO TEXAS® JACKPOT ADVERTISED AT $19 MILLION FROM JUNE 15 DRAWING
(Austin) ­ The JALA Revocable Trust, Lowell A. Johnson, Trustee, of Calvert today claimed the June 15 Lotto Texas® jackpot advertised at $19 million. Purchased with the Cash Value Option, the ticket is not a Quick Pick and has a lump-sum value of $12.8 million, before taxes. The numbers drawn were 3-13-14-17-31-33. The winner also claimed an additional $41 won on another Lotto Texas ticket for the same drawing. Mike's Country Store, located at 209 N. Main in Calvert, sold both tickets. Selling the jackpotwinning ticket makes this retailer eligible to receive a retailer bonus equal to one percent of the advertised jackpot. That comes to $190,000. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $99 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $16 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $180 million. More information about the Texas Lottery® is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
