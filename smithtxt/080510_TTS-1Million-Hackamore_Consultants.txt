TEXAS TWO STEP® PRIZE OF $1 MILLION CLAIMED BY DALLAS-AREA WINNER
(Austin) ­Hackamore Consultants, LLC, by Southern Sky Investments, Inc. by Charlotte Christina Rose, Sole Shareholder, of Dallas has collected a Texas Two Step® top prize of $1 million.

The win came from the drawing held June 24. This ticket matched the four numbers drawn, 5-1830-33, and the Bonus Ball number, 15. Independence Shell, 2900 W. 15th Street in Plano, sold the ticket. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket.

The winner requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $53 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $5 million, and Powerball® is an estimated $25 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
