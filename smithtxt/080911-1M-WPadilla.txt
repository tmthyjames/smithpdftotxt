CYPRESS MAN WINS $1 MILLION TOP PRIZE IN $500 MILLION FRENZY SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(Austin) ­ Walter Padilla of Cypress claimed the seventh of 40 $1 million prizes in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game $500 Million Frenzy. With more than half a billion dollars in total prizes, the $500 Million Frenzy boasts overall odds of winning any prize of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Padilla's win leaves 33 secondtier prizes remaining unclaimed. In addition, nine top prizes worth $2.5 million each currently remain unclaimed. For selling the winning ticket, the E Z Food Store, 9941 Long Point Road in Houston, is eligible to receive a retailer bonus of $10,000. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $12 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $220 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $17 million.
