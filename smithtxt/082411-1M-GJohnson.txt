EIGHTH $1 MILLION TOP PRIZE CLAIMED IN

$500 MILLION FRENZY SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(Austin) ­Gary T Johnson of Austin claimed a $1 million prize in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game $500 Million Frenzy. The Randalls #1779, 2301 Ranch Road 620 South in Austin, sold Johnson his lucky $500 Million Frenzy ticket. The store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus for selling a winning ticket worth $1 million. The game features more than half a billion dollars in total prizes and overall odds of winning any prize of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. The $500 Million Frenzy has nine $2.5 million prizes and 32 $1 million prizes still unclaimed. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $14 million. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $47 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $21 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, visit www.txlottery.org. ###
