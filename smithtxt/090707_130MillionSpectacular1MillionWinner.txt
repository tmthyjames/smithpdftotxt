HOUSTON RESIDENT WINS $1 MILLION PLAYING $130 MILLION SPECTACULAR
(AUSTIN) ­ Joseph N. Barras I of Houston has claimed a second-tier prize worth $1 million in $130 Million Spectacular from the Texas Lottery®. He purchased the ticket at the Mercury Drive-In, 1014 Mercury in Jacinto City. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for having sold the ticket. $130 Million Spectacular costs $50 and went on sale May 15. Today's winner claimed the fourth of six second-tier prizes the game offers. There are two top prizes of $5 million that remain unclaimed in this game. Mr. Barras requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $17 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $23 million.
