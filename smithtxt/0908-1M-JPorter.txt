FIRST PRIZE OF $1 MILLION AWARDED IN TEXAS

LOTTERY® BLACK-SERIES III-LIMITED EDITION
(Austin) ­ The first $1 million top prize has been awarded in the Texas Lottery® BlackSeries III-Limited Edition scratch-off game. Joel P. Porter of North Richland Hills purchased the winning ticket at the Kroger #582, 9135 Grapevine Highway, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. Porter requested minimal publicity. Introduced in July, the Texas Lottery® Black-Series III-Limited Edition features more than $44 million in total prizes and overall odds of winning any prize of one in 3.32, including break-even prizes. Five top prizes of $1 million each remain unclaimed. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $44 million. Saturday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $4 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, please visit www.txlottery.org. ###
