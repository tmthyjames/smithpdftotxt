ARLINGTON MAN TWO-STEPS TO $1 MILLION JACKPOT
(AUSTIN) ­ Bradley R. Horton of Arlington matched all four numbers and the Bonus Ball to win a $1 million Texas Two Step® prize. He purchased a Quick Pick ticket at the EZ Mart located at 3785 SW Green Oaks in Arlington for the Sept. 9 drawing. The winning numbers were 4-721-24, Bonus Ball 2.

Texas Two Step is played by selecting four numbers from one to 35 and by choosing one Bonus Ball number from one to 35. Players can play their numbers for up to 10 drawings in advance. Each play costs $1. Texas Two Step drawings take place every Monday and Thursday at 10:12 p.m. CDT. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 32. The claimant requested minimum publicity. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $41 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $15 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance. The Texas Lottery provides several entertaining games for lottery players including Powerball®, Mega Millions®, Lotto Texas®, All or NothingTM, Texas Two Step®, Pick 3TM, Daily 4TM, Cash Five® and scratch-offs.
