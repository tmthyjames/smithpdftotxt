$34,226 MAKES TRIP TO AUSTIN MORE THAN WORTH THE DRIVE FOR SAN MARCOS CASH FIVE® CLAIMANT
(AUSTIN) ­ When a store clerk told Doris Arrington of San Marcos that she would need to visit a Texas Lottery® claim center to collect her prize from the September 1 Cash Five® drawing, she assumed she must have matched four of five numbers and could expect a payout of several hundred dollars. Imagine her surprise when claim center personnel broke the news that she had in fact matched all five numbers in that drawing and that she was due $34,226! "I was more than happy to drive up here for a few hundred dollars," Arrington explained. "But when they told me how much I had actually won, I was so surprised I was just speechless." Arrington said she and her husband always play the same numbers, based on family birthdays, but the most they'd ever won before was about $100. Ms. Arrington said she thought she and her husband would probably invest her winnings, but there was one little piece of business she had to take care of first. "I'm going to go see him at work to break the good news," Arrington said. "I could call him, but then I wouldn't get to see the look on his face when he finds out!" "I'm sure Ms. Arrington's husband will be happy to see her and I congratulate her on her good luck," said Anthony J. Sadberry, executive director of the Texas Lottery Commission.
