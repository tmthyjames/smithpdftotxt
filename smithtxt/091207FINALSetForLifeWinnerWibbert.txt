HOUSTON TRUST CLAIMS TOP PRIZE IN SET FOR LIFE FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­The Wibbert Revocable Trust, Dale Wibbert, trustee, of Houston claimed the top prize in Set For Life, a Texas Lottery® scratch-off ticket with a top prize of $5,000 per week for 20 years, not to exceed $5 million. This is the first of the three top prizes to be claimed in this game. Wibbert purchased the ticket at the Handi Stop #19, 9721 Jones Road in Houston. For having sold this ticket, the retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000. Mr. Wibbert requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $36 million.
