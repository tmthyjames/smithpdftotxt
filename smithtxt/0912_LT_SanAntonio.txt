SAN ANTONIO TRUST CLAIMS $15.75 MILLION LOTTO TEXAS® JACKPOT FROM AUGUST

(AUSTIN) ­ Piedmont Sycamore Trust, Dustin Whittenburg, Trustee, of San Antonio, claimed the $10,671,627.75 Cash Value Option for the $15.75 million estimated annuitized Lotto Texas® jackpot from Aug. 16. The trust selected all six numbers drawn (1-2-4-27-40-42) on a ticket purchased at the Texas Sun located at 3263 Roosevelt Ave. in San Antonio. For selling the winning ticket the store is eligible for a retailer bonus of $157,500.

Lotto Texas drawings are held every Wednesday and Saturday evening at 10:12 p.m. Players select six numbers from one to 54 for the chance to win Lotto Texas. For $1 more per play, the Extra! add-on feature provides a chance to increase non-jackpot prizes up to $10,000.

The claimant requested minimal publicity. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $44 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $16 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
