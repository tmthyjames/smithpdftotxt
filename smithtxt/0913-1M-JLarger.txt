SAN ANTONIO RESIDENT OPTS FOR MEGAPLIER® AND WINS $1 MILLION IN MEGA MILLIONS® DRAW
(Austin) ­ James P. Larger of San Antonio didn't need to match the Mega Ball number in Friday's Mega Millions® drawing to win a significant prize, because he selected the Megaplier® option when he purchased his ticket. As a result, his second-tier prize automatically became a cool $1 million. Larger won his prize in the Mega Millions drawing held September 9. His decision to Megaply® for $1 extra bumped his $250,000 prize up to $1 million. The winning ticket was purchased at the Rams Express located at 4415 Ramsgate in San Antonio. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions jackpot is an estimated $54 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $25 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $5 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit www.txlottery.org. ###
