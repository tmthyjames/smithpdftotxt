MAN FROM ROCKPORT WINS $1 MILLION PLAYING POWERBALL® LAST SATURDAY
(Austin) ­ Robert A. Nelson Jr. of Rockport today claimed a prize of $1 million in last Saturday's Powerball® drawing. The second-tier Powerball prize is worth $200,000. Nelson also purchased the Power Play® option, which multiplied the match-five prize by five, making his prize worth $1 million. The numbers drawn were 1-18-37-39-44, and the Powerball number was 13.

Nelson purchased the ticket at the Speedy Stop Food Store #11, Highway 35 N and Park Road #13, in Rockport.

The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $5 million, and the Powerball is an estimated $20 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $16 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
