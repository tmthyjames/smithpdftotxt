SAN ANTONIO RESIDENT CLAIMS $1,175,000 TEXAS TWO STEP® PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Victor G. Posada, of San Antonio, claimed a Texas Two Step jackpot prize worth $1,175,000 for the drawing held on Sept. 22. The winning ticket was purchased at Mireles, Inc. located at 3630 Culebra Rd. in San Antonio.

For selling the winning ticket, the retailer is eligible for a $10,000 bonus.

Mr. Posada requested minimum publicity.

Texas Two Step jackpots start at $200,000 and increase each drawing until there is a winner. Texas Two Step drawings are held on Mondays and Thursdays at 10:12 p.m. Central Time. The game features overall odds of 1 in 32.4.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $22 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $44 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $17 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas.
