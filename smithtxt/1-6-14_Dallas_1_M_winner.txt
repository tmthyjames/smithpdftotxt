TWO DALLAS-AREA TEXAS LOTTERY® RETAILERS SELL $1 MILLION TOP PRIZES IN 20X CA$H GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ Robert D. Morgan of Dallas claimed a $1 million prize in the 20X Ca$h game yesterday. This is the second 20X Ca$h top prize to be sold in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. Morgan purchased his ticket at the Stop N Shop located at 401 E. Pioneer in Irving. The other $1 million prize-winning ticket came from the EZ Mart #489, 100 E. Bardin in Arlington, and was claimed in November 2013.

With more than $76 million in total prizes, the 20X Ca$h scratch-off game features overall odds of one in 3.05. Three top prizes of $1 million each currently remain unclaimed in the game.

The Stop N Shop is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus for selling the winning ticket.

This claimant requested minimal publicity.
