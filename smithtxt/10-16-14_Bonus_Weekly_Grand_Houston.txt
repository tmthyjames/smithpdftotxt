HOUSTON RESIDENT CLAIMS $2.6 MILLION BONUS WEEKLY GRAND TOP PRIZE

(AUSTIN) ­ María Guadalupe García De Muñoz, of Houston, claimed a Bonus Weekly Grand top prize ticket worth $2,500 per week for 20 years (not to exceed $2,600,000). For selling the winning ticket, the Handi Plus 273 located at 2490 S. Wayside Drive in Houston is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

With more than $51 million in total prizes, the Bonus Weekly Grand features overall odds of winning of one in 3.96, including break-even prizes. Three top prizes and millions of prizes from $5 to $20,000 currently remain unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
