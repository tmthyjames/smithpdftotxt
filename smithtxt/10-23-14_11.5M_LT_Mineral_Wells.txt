LOTTO TEXAS® WINNER FROM MINERAL WELLS CLAIMS JACKPOT PRIZE FROM OCT. 21
(AUSTIN) ­ Mineral Wells resident Monica Fielding claimed the $8,036,856.68 Cash Value of the estimated $11.5 million Lotto Texas® jackpot for Oct. 15. Fielding's ticket matched all six numbers drawn (8-21-32-35-45-52) on Oct. 15. She purchased her Quick Pick ticket at the Kwik Stop #3, 1607 S E 22nd Ave. in Mineral Wells. For selling the winning ticket, the retailer is eligible for a $115,000 retailer bonus.

Lotto Texas is played by selecting six numbers from one to 54. Each play is only $1. Players can also add on the Extra! feature for a chance to increase non-jackpot prizes up to $10,000.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
