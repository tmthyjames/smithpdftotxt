HOUSTON RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION WITH THE TEXAS LOTTERY® GAME $200,000,000 CASH BLOWOUT

(AUSTIN) ­ Liliana Garcia of Pasadena claimed a $200,000,000 Cash Blowout top prize winning ticket worth $1,000,000. Garcia purchased the ticket at the Maxey Shell located at 570 Maxey Road in Houston. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

This is the second of 10 top prizes to be claimed in the $200,000,000 Cash Blowout. The game features a total of more than $200 million in prizes and overall odds of winning of one in 2.82, including break-even prizes.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
