MEGAPLIER® STRIKES AGAIN, SPRING RESIDENT
CLAIMS $1 MILLION MEGA MILLIONS® PRIZE
(Austin) ­ Spring resident John H. Smith III claimed a $1 million Mega Millions® prize yesterday at the Texas Lottery® claim center in Houston. Smith's ticket matched all five numbers (3 ­ 19 ­ 21 ­ 44 ­ 45), but not the Mega Ball (29) drawn on September 30. Since Smith spent an extra $1 to Megaply® his ticket, what would have been a $250,000 5-of-5 prize increased to a guaranteed $1 million. The winning ticket was purchased at the Corner Food Mart located at 4634 Irvington Blvd. in Houston. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions jackpot is an estimated $12 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $58 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $11 million.
