TOP PRIZE OF $7.5 MILLION IN ULTIMATE CASINO JACKPOT SCRATCH-OFF CLAIMED
(Austin) ­John Clark of Austin today claimed the first of the three top prizes of $7.5 million offered in Ultimate Casino Jackpot, a Texas Lottery® scratch-off game.

Clark purchased the ticket at the Rattler's Country Store #7, 2050 Holleman Drive West in College Station. The store is eligible to receive a retailer bonus of $10,000 for selling this ticket.

Ultimate Casino Jackpot is a $50-ticket and was issued in late March this year. The game has numerous levels of second-tier prizes beginning at $10,000 down to the break-even prize.

Clark requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $61 million. The Lotto Texas® jackpot for Wednesday is an estimated $11 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $25 million.
