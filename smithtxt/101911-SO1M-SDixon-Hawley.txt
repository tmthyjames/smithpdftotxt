FIRST $1 MILLION PRIZE AWARDED IN RECENTLY INTRODUCED MERRY MILLIONAIRE SCRATCH-OFF
(Austin) ­ This month Texas Lottery® scratch-off games with holiday themes began appearing on store shelves, and one player got an early present. Susan Dixon of Hawley claimed the first of three $1 million top prizes in the Merry Millionaire game. The Merry Millionaire game features more than $31 million in total prizes. Two top prizes worth $1 million each and 12 second-tier prizes of $10,000 remain unclaimed. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 3.07. Dixon purchased her winning ticket at the 7-Eleven #22, 1657 Highway 351 in Abilene, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $124 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $15 million. Tomorrow's Texas Two Step® drawing features an advertised jackpot of $1.05 million. This claimant requested minimal publicity.
