PLAYER FROM BOERNE CLAIMS $1 MILLION TOP PRIZE IN $200 MILLION CASH SPECTACULAR GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ Richard A. Peck of Boerne can celebrate winning "all that cash" after claiming a $1 million top prize in the $200 Million Cash Spectacular game from the Texas Lottery®. Peck purchased his ticket at the Valero Corner Store #1058, making the retailer eligible for a $10,000 bonus. With more than $200 million in total prizes, the $200 Million Cash Spectacular features overall odds of winning of one in 3.56, including break-even prizes. Only one $1 million top prize currently remains unclaimed. Other top-prize winning tickets have been purchased in Yoakum, Vidor, Pearsall, Bellmead, Odessa, San Antonio, Dickinson and Cameron. This claimant requested minimum publicity. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $41 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $15 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance. The Texas Lottery provides several entertaining games for lottery players including Powerball®, Mega Millions®, Lotto Texas®, All or NothingTM, Texas Two Step®, Pick 3TM, Daily 4TM, Cash Five® and scratch-offs.
