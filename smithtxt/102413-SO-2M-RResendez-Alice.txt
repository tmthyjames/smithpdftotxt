TEXAS LOTTERY® PLAYER FROM ALICE MAKES IT GRAND WITH $2.6 MILLION BONUS WEEKLY GRAND TOP PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Rene Resendez of Alice claimed a Bonus Weekly Grand top prize of $2,500 per week for 20 years, a total of $2.6 million before taxes. This is the second of four top prizes to be awarded in the game since it was introduced on Sept. 9. The Bonus Weekly Grand is part of a new suite of Weekly Grand-themed Texas Lottery® scratch-off games. It features more than $52 million in total prizes, and odds of winning any prize are one in 3.96. The Stripes #7134, located at 102 S. Reynolds in Orange Grove, sold the winning ticket, making the store eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000 This claimant requested minimum publicity. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $41 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $15 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance. The Texas Lottery provides several entertaining games for lottery players including Powerball®, Mega Millions®, Lotto Texas®, All or NothingTM, Texas Two Step®, Pick 3TM, Daily 4TM, Cash Five® and scratch-offs.
