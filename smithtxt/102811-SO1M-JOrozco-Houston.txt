THIRD $1 MILLION PRIZE AWARDED IN TEXAS LOTTERY® BLACK-SERIES III-LIMITED EDITION
(Austin) ­ Introduced in July, the third version of the Texas Lottery® Black game just awarded its third top prize of $1 million. Jose Orozco of Houston claimed the prize at the Texas Lottery® claim center in Houston today. With more than $44 million in total prizes, the Texas Lottery® Black-Series III-Limited Edition game features overall odds of winning of one in 3.32, including break even prizes. Three top prizes of $1 million each and nine $10,000 second-tier prizes remain unclaimed. The E-Z Food Store located at 14648 Walters Road #102 in Houston is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus for selling the winning ticket. This claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $67 million. Tomorrow's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $203 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $18 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit www.txlottery.org. ###
