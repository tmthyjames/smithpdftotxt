Lotto Texas® Estimated Jackpot For Saturday, June 11 Drawing Remains At $8 Million
(Austin, June 8) - The Texas Lottery Commission today announced that the estimated jackpot for the Saturday, June 11, 2005, Lotto Texas® drawing will remain at $8 million, provided no tickets match all five numbers and Bonus Ball in tonight's drawing. Current sales for the Lotto Texas® game, along with the current interest rate factor, do not support an increase in the estimated jackpot amount at this time. Overall, Texas Lottery sales for all games for the current fiscal year are ahead 10.6 percent compared to the same time period last year. ... The Texas Lottery Commission supports public education by contributing to the Foundation School Fund.
