SAN ANTONIO RESIDENT CLAIMS SUPER WEEKLY GRAND TOP PRIZE WORTH $5.2 MILLION

(AUSTIN) ­ Kenneth Earl Pittman of San Antonio claimed a Super Weekly Grand top prize worth $5,000 per week for 20 years, a total of $5.2 million. For selling the winning ticket, the S V EZ One Stop at 4758 Old Seguin Road in Kirby is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

Super Weekly Grand features more than $84 million in total prizes and overall odds of winning of one in 3.97, including break-even prizes. Two top prizes worth $5.2 million each and millions of prizes from $10 to $1,000 currently remain unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
