CROCKETT RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION SCRATCH-OFF PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Robert R. Thompson of Crockett claimed a 20X Ca$h top prize worth $1 million. The ticket was purchased at the Trinity Diamond Mart located at 607 South Robb in Trinity. The store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity.

20X Ca$h features more than $76.9 million in total prizes. The overall odds of winning are one in 3.05, including break-even prizes. Thompson claimed the fifth of the five $1 million top prizes in the game.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $22 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $44 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $17 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
