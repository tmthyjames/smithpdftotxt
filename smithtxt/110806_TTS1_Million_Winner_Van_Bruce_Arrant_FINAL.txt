WINNER COLLECTS $1.075 MILLION TEXAS TWO STEP® OCTOBER 2ND PRIZE FROM THE TEXAS LOTTERY®
(AUSTIN) ­ Van Bruce Arrant of Colleyville on Monday collected a $1.075 million Texas Two Step® prize from the Texas Lottery®. The drawing for this prize occurred on October 2. There were two tickets sold that matched all the numbers drawn. Those were 6-20-28-31, and the bonus ball was 23. The other ticket was sold in Bryan at the Exxon Express Mart, 515 N. Harvey Mitchell Parkway. Arrant purchased his ticket at the Mid-Cities Beverages, 901 E. Mid-Cities Boulevard in Euless. The store is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for having sold the ticket that matched the numbers drawn. The winner requested minimum publicity. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www.txlottery.org
