DALLAS RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION PRIZE AND A LITTLE MORE FROM FRIDAY'S MEGA MILLIONS® DRAWING
(Austin) ­ Stella Taylor of Dallas today claimed a prize of $1,002,400 from the Mega Millions® drawing last Friday. Taylor selected the numbers on her tickets and also purchased the Megaplier® feature. The Mega Ball number was four, which turned Taylor's megaplied base prize of $250,000 into $1 million. She purchased several tickets for the drawing, which brought her an additional $2,400.

Taylor bought her tickets at the Todora Parkit Market, 4724 Greenville Avenue, Dallas. Hers was one of two second-tier Mega Millions tickets sold Friday. The other ticket was purchased in San Antonio without the Megaplier feature.

The numbers selected in last Friday's drawing were 46-19-34-25-53, and the Mega Ball was 15. She purchased the tickets approximately 15 minutes before sales closed for the day at 9:45 p.m.

Taylor called this win, "a true blessing." The winner requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions jackpot is an estimated $25 million. The Lotto Texas® jackpot for Wednesday is an estimated $6 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million.
