BAYTOWN RESIDENT CLAIMS PRIZE WORTH $1 MILLION IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER GAME
(Austin) ­ Cyrille Donelly of Baytown this week claimed a prize worth $1 million in the $500,000,000 Blockbuster scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery®.

The game offers 10 top prizes of $2.5 million, six of which have been claimed. Donelly's prize of $1 million is one of 40 in the game, and 16 of those remain.

Valero Corner Store #2007, 2219 Highway 201 in Baytown, sold the ticket. This makes the retailer eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000.

Donelly requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $8 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $35 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $25 million. There is more information about the Texas Lottery available on the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
