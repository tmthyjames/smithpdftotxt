TEXAS LOTTERY ANNOUNCES APPARENT SUCCESSFUL PROPOSER FOR LOTTERY OPERATOR CONTRACT

(Austin)-The Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) announced today that GTECH Corporation has been named the Apparent Successful Proposer for the Lottery Operations and Services Request for Proposals, which was issued in January 2010. In addition to GTECH Corporation, proposals were also submitted by INTRALOT, Inc. and Scientific Games International, Inc. The TLC will immediately enter into contract negotiations with GTECH Corporation, with the goal being to reach an agreement prior to the end of the calendar year. Except for certain documents involving proprietary information and lottery security, all documents related to this procurement that are subject to open records requirements in Texas will be released once a contract has been signed.
