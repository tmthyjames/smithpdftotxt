ANGLETON LIMITED PARTNERSHIP CLAIMS $18 MILLION LOTTO TEXAS® JACKPOT
(AUSTIN) ­ DLL Properties, LP, By DLL Investment, LLC, Donald R. Ludeman, President, of Angleton today claimed the $18 million jackpot prize from the November 11 Lotto Texas® drawing. Since the claimant chose the Cash Value Option, the total amount of the prize before taxes is $10,968,234.45. "On behalf of the Texas Lottery Commission, I'm happy to congratulate our sixth Lotto Texas claimant of 2006," said Anthony J. Sadberry, executive director of the Texas Lottery. "I'd also like to take this opportunity to wish everyone a happy and safe Thanksgiving holiday." The winning ticket was purchased at the Handi Plus #45, located at 2301 W. Highway 35 in Angleton. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $181,122.69 for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $16 million and Wednesday's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $6 million.
