HOUSTON SCRATCH-OFF PLAYER CLAIMS $1 MILLION TEXAS LOTTERY® BLACK TOP PRIZE
(Austin) ­ Houston resident Constance E. Dillard claimed a $1 million Texas Lottery® Black Series III-Limited Edition top prize at the Texas Lottery® claim center in Houston. With more than $44 million in total prizes, the Texas Lottery® Black-Series III-Limited Edition game features overall odds of winning of one in 3.32, including break even prizes. As of today, two top prizes of $1 million each and six $10,000 second-tier prizes remain unclaimed. The ticket was purchased at the Fiesta Mart #18 located at 8130 Kirby Dr. in Houston. The retailer is eligible to receive a $10,000 bonus for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $25 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $55 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit www.txlottery.org. ###
