THIRD $2.5 MILLION TOP PRIZE IN $500 MILLION

FRENZY CLAIMED BY PLAYER FROM CARLSBAD
(Austin) ­ There may be a frenzy of activity at the Pic-N-Pac located at 5904 N. US Highway 87 in San Angelo when news gets out that the retailer sold a $500 Million Frenzy scratch-off ticket worth $2.5 million. Carlton Ray Luckett of Carlsbad claimed the prize at Texas Lottery® headquarters in Austin yesterday and requested minimal publicity. For selling the winning ticket, the Pic-NPac is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. With more than half-a-billion dollars in total prizes, the $500 Million Frenzy boasts overall odds of winning any prize of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Currently, seven top prizes of $2.5 million and 31 second-tier prizes of $1 million remain unclaimed. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $64 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $31 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $27 million.
