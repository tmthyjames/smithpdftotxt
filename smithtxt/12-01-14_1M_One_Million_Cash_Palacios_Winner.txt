PALACIOS RESIDENT CLAIMS ONE MILLION CASH $1 MILLION DOLLAR TOP PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Qui Tran of Palacios claimed a $1 million top prize from the Texas Lottery® scratchoff game One Million Cash. The ticket was purchased at the Palacios Drive-In located at 1111 Henderson in Palacios. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

One Million Cash features more than $88 million in total prizes and overall odds of winning are one in 2.80 including break-even prizes. Tran claimed the second $1 million top prize in the game with two more remaining.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $22 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $44 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $17 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
