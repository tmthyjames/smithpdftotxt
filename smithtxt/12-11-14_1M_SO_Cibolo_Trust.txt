CIBOLO TRUST CLAIMS $1 MILLION PRIZE IN THE $200,000,000 CASH BLOWOUT SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ The Jerome and Sherry Rakowitz Living Trust claimed the third of 10 $1 million top prizes offered in the $200,000,000 Cash Blowout game. For selling the winning ticket, the Star Mart located at 14462 Interstate 10 E. in Marion is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.

With more than $200 million in total prizes, overall odds of winning any prize in the $200,000,000 Cash Blowout are one in 2.82, including break-even prizes. In addition to the seven remaining $1 million top prizes, millions of prizes from $10 to $100,000 currently remain unclaimed. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $22 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $44 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $17 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
