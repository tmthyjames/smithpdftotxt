DALLAS RESIDENT CLAIMS $2.5 MILLION SCRATCHOFF PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Dallas resident Gregg S Biba won a $2.5 million top prize in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game 100X The Cash. Biba claimed the sixth of the eight top prizes available in the game.

With more than $183 million in total prizes, the 100X The Cash game features overall odds of winning of one in 2.86, including break-even prizes.

The ticket was purchased at C Store #12 at 4040 Cedar Springs in Dallas. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.

About the Texas Lottery

Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $22 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $44 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $17 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.

The Texas Lottery provides several entertaining games for lottery players including Powerball®, Mega Millions®, Lotto Texas®, All or NothingTM, Texas Two Step®, Pick 3TM, Daily 4TM, Cash Five® and scratch-offs. For more information visit us on Facebook, Twitter, YouTube or at www.txlottery.org.
