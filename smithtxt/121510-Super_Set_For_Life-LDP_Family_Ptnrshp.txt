HOUSTON PARTNERSHIP CLAIMS TOP PRIZE OF $500,000 ANNUALLY FOR 15 YEARS IN SUPER SET FOR LIFE GAME
(Austin) ­ LDP Family Partnership, L.P., by LDP Interests, L.L.C., General Partner, by Robert R. Akeroyd, President, of Houston, today claimed the top prize of $500,000 per year for 15 years in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off, Super Set For Life. "God gave me a blessing and I promised Him it would not be squandered," Akeroyd said. Akeroyd bought the ticket at the Sunmart #141, 14711 S. Main Street, Suite A, in Houston. The store is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling this top-prize-winning ticket. "We issued this version of Super Set For Life in July of this year," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Gary Grief. "This is the first of the game's four top prizes to be claimed. The game also offers second-tier prizes beginning at $10,000 and going through numerous prize levels from $2,000 down to the break-even amount of $20." The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $6 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $123 million. More information about the Texas Lottery is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
