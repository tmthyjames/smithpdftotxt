WESLACO MAN WINS WEEKLY BONUS TOP PRIZE OF $500 PER WEEK FOR 20 YEARS
(Austin) ­ Raul Ramirez of Weslaco today claimed the top prize in Weekly Bonus, a Texas Lottery® scratch-off ticket that will give him $500 per week for 20 years.

"This is the last of the five top prizes to be claimed in this game," said Texas Lottery Commission Deputy Executive Director Gary Grief. "As a result, we will now be closing this game."

Ramirez bought the ticket at Bob's Drive-Thru, 415 Highway 83 East, in Alamo. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $140 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $21 million.
