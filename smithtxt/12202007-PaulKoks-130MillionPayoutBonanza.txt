$50-TICKET, $130 MILLION PAYOUT BONANZA, BRINGS HOUSTON-AREA MAN $5 MILLION TOP PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Paul Koks of the The Woodlands this week collected the top prize of $5 million after buying a ticket in $130 Million Payout Bonanza, a $50-scratch-off ticket from the Texas Lottery®. Koks purchased the ticket at the S&A Grocery, 25226 Grogans Mill Road, in The Woodlands. The store is eligible for a $10,000 bonus for selling the winning ticket. The new winner requested minimal publicity. This is the first of the three top prizes the game offers to be claimed. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $12 million.
