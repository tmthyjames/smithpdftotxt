$1 MILLION WIN JUST IN TIME FOR THE HOLIDAYS
(Austin) ­ One Amarillo resident's holidays just got a little merrier. Marlon Boyd claimed the tenth $1 million prize in the $500 Million Frenzy game on Tuesday. Designed with more than half a billion dollars in total prizes, the $500 Million Frenzy has seven prizes of $2.5 million and 30 prizes of $1 million remaining unclaimed. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Boyd's winning ticket was purchased from the Toot N Totum #1, 1500 S. Washington St. in Amarillo, making the store eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $173 million. Saturday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $125 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $7 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, visit www.txlottery.org. ###
