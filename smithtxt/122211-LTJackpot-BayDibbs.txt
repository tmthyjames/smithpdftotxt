$30 MILLION ADVERTISED LOTTO TEXAS® PRIZE FROM DECEMBER 10 CLAIMED
(Austin) ­ Bay Dibbs, Ltd., Bay Dibbs Management, L.L.C., General Partner, Shirley Christy of Seabrook, Executive Vice-President, claimed the Lotto Texas® prize advertised at $30 million for the drawing held December 10. The winning ticket matched all six numbers drawn, 4 ­ 5 ­ 14 ­ 16 ­ 18 ­ 19. Because the cash value option was selected and another set of numbers matched for a second prize, the ticket is worth $22,448,238, before taxes. Hill's #2 located at 2304 A Bayport Blvd. in Seabrook sold the winning ticket, making the store eligible for a $300,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $173 million. Saturday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $125 million, and the Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $7 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery®, visit www.txlottery.org. ###
