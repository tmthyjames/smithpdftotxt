FIRST PLATINUM PRESENT TOP PRIZE WORTH $1 MILLION CLAIMED BY MISSION RESIDENT
(AUSTIN) ­ Gary P. Cain of Mission claimed the first of three Platinum Present top prizes worth $1 million each and requested minimal publicity. Introduced in October, the Platinum Present scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery® features more than $37 million in total prizes. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.99, including break-even prizes. This winning ticket was purchased at the Stripes No. 2265, 1600 E. Griffin Parkway in Mission, making the store eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $196 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $10 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org.
