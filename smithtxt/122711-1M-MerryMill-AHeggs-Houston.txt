FINAL $1 MILLION MERRY MILLIONAIRE TOP PRIZE CLAIMED BY HOUSTON SCRATCH-OFF PLAYER
(Austin) ­ Last Friday, Alvin R. Heggs of Houston claimed the third of three $1 million prizes in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game Merry Millionaire. With more than $31 million in total prizes, the $20 Merry Millionaire features overall odds of winning any prize of one in 3.07, including break-even prizes. Though Heggs claimed the final $1 million top prize, six second-tier prizes of $10,000 currently remain unclaimed. Heggs purchased his ticket at Shop N Go #9 located at 1999 FM 1960 West Rd. in Houston. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $8 million. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $12 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, visit www.txlottery.org. ###
