Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - February 2, 2005

Texas Lottery® Announces Return Of Set For Life Scratch Off Game
(Austin, February 2) - Beginning today, Texas Lottery® players can find the popular Set For Life scratch off game back in lottery retail outlets. Set For Life (game #533) replaces Set For Life (game #499), which was recalled on January 13, 2005, by the Texas Lottery Commission. For $10 a ticket, Set For Life offers players the opportunity to win the top prize ­ $5,000 a week (not to exceed $5 million total). The game also offers more than 257,000 prizes of $50 to $500. Each ticket offers players 20 chances to win. The odds of winning any prize are one in 3.47, including break-even prizes. In addition to two top prizes, Set For Life also offers prizes in the following amounts: $2,500, $1,000, $500, $200, $100, $50, $20 and $10. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www.txlottery.org ... The Texas Lottery supports public education by contributing to the Foundation School Fund.
