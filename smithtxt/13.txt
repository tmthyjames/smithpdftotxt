Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - October 26, 2001 Contact: Karen Kalergis,, 512-344-5436

Tonight's Texas Two StepTM Jackpot Sets A New Record
(Austin, October 26) - - Tonight's Texas Two Step jackpot is the highest in the new games five-month history, according to Texas Lottery Commission On-Line Product Manager Robert Tirloni. "Tonight's jackpot is worth $1.6 million," said Tirloni. "That's the highest it's ever been since the game started five months ago." Tirloni said if there is no winner tonight, the jackpot will roll to $1.9 million for Tuesday night's drawing, setting another new record. For a one dollar ticket, players try to match four numbers from a field of 35, and a Bonus Ball number from a second field of 35 in order to win the jackpot. Since the game began in May of this year, it has generated more than $45 million in sales. Tonight's drawing will be held at 9:59 p.m. The drawings are broadcast live from the Texas Lottery's Drawings Studio at 611 East 6th Street in Austin. Drawings results are also available on the web site www.txlottery.org. - 30 .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
