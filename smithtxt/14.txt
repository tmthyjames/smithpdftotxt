Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - October 26, 2001 Contact: Leticia Vasquez,, 512-344-5208

$40 Million Lotto Texas Jackpot Ticket Sold In Round Rock Claimed
(Austin, October 26) - - The Hermosillo Family Trust today claimed the $40 million Lotto Texas jackpot for the drawing on October 3, 2001. Ramon Hermosillo is the trustee. The winning Quick Pick ticket was purchased with a Cash Value Option, which gives the winner the net present value of the jackpot, approximately half of the advertised jackpot. The winner's prize totaled $23,212,912, before taxes. For selling the big prize-winner, the Sam Bass Road Shell, located at 806 Sam Bass Road in Round Rock, is eligible to receive a bonus of one percent of the advertised jackpot, which comes to $400,000. The winning numbers were 36-32-38-46-12-9. The trust requested minimal publicity. Saturday's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $24 million. -30.... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
