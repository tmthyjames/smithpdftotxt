Texas Lottery Commission Announces Formation Of Executive Director Search Committee
(Austin, July 22) - The Texas Lottery Commission today announced the formation of an Executive Director Search Committee to assist and advise the three-member commission in identifying qualified candidates. The members of the Texas Lottery Executive Director Search Committee are: C. Tom Clowe Jr., Texas Lottery Commission chairman; Professor Gerald Busald, San Antonio College; John Edwards, executive director of the State Bar of Texas; David Heinlein, Charitable Bingo industry representative; Francisco Hernandez, Texas Ethics Commission member; Rick Johnson, president of the Texas Grocery & Convenience Association; Dawn Nettles, publisher of the Lotto Report; Anthony Sadberry, former Texas Lottery commissioner; Leticia Vásquez, Texas Lottery Commission employee; and Elizabeth Whitaker, former Texas Lottery commissioner. Committee members will serve on a strictly voluntary basis and will not be compensated for their time or expenses. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www.txlottery.org. ...
