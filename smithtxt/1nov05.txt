Temple Scratch-Off Player Claims 74th Weekly Grand Top Prize
(Austin, November 30) - Kenneth A. Eichmann of Temple today claimed the top prize ­ $1,000 a week for 20 years ­ in the Texas Lottery® Weekly Grand scratch-off game. The total amount of the prize before taxes is $1,040,000. "I really want to congratulate Mr. Eichmann for his Weekly Grand good fortune," said Robert Tirloni, products manager for the Texas Lottery Commission. "Weekly Grand has been one of our top selling scratch-off games for more than a decade now and, after 74 winners, it's as popular as ever." The winning ticket was purchased at the Mickey's Convenience #23, located at 200 East FM 2410 in Harker Heights. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $25 million.
