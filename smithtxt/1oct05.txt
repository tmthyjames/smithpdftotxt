Georgetown Resident Collects $1 Million Cashes In On Second Viva Las Vegas Top Prize
(Austin, October 31) - William Norman Vandervort became the second winner to collect the $1 million top prize from the Texas Lottery® VIVA LAS VEGAS scratch game. The Georgetown resident purchased the winning ticket last Friday and claimed the prize today at Texas Lottery Commission headquarters in Austin. "We attempted to bring some of that Las Vegas fun and excitement to Texas with the VIVA LAS VEGAS game, but without the hassle of buying a plane ticket," said Robert Tirloni, products manager for the Texas Lottery Commission. "The VIVA LAS VEGAS game offers players great overall odds of winning any prize, 1 in 2.79, with five different games to play and up to 24 times to win." Vandervort purchased his ticket at Quick Stop, located at 5700 Williams Drive in Georgetown. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the topprize winning ticket. As of today, there is still a $1 million prize available to claim. Other cash prizes for the VIVA LAS VEGAS scratch game range from $20 to $10,000.
