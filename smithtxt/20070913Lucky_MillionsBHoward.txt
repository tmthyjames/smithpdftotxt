LUCKY CAMERON WOMAN WINS MILLIONS
(AUSTIN) ­ Brenda Howard of Cameron visited the Texas Lottery Commission today to claim a top-prize in the Lucky Millions scratch-off game. This is the second $3 million winning-ticket claimed in the $30 game. Odds of winning any prize, including break-even amounts, are one in 2.26. Davids Exxon at 1801 W. 2nd Street in Taylor is eligible to receive a $10,000 bonus for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $36 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $26 million.
