$1 MILLION? IT'S A DEAL FOR BEAUMONT RESIDENT
(AUSTIN) ­ Jessie L Walters of Beaumont claimed a top-prize today in the Deal or No Deal scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery®. She will receive $50,000 each year for 20 years, a total of $1 million. Two top prizes remain unclaimed in this game. For selling the winning ticket, the Little Market, located at 1094 Center St. in Beaumont, is eligible to receive a $10,000 bonus. The winner requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $12 million. Tonight's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $6 million.
