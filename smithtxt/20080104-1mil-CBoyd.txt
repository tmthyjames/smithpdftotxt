$130 MILLION PAYOUT BONANZA PAYS OUT ITS FIRST $1 MILLION PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Sweetwater got a surprise today when local resident Calvin Boyd became the latest $1 million winner from the Texas Lottery® by playing the $130 Million Payout Bonanza scratch-off game. Boyd's prize was the first second-tier prize claimed in Texas' newest $50 instant ticket game. Two $5 million prizes and five $1 million prizes remain. The claimant requested minimal publicity. The 7-Eleven located at 395 E. Second in Colorado City is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus for selling the winning ticket. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $12 million.
