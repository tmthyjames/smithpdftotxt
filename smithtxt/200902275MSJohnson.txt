LOTTERY PLAYER FROM SEGUIN WINS $5 MILLION TOP PRIZE FROM $130,000,000 PLATINUM PAYOUT
(AUSTIN) ­ Texas Lottery® scratch-off player Stephanie C Johnson of Seguin has 5 million reasons to celebrate. She claimed the first of three top prizes in the $130,000,000 Platinum Payout instant game. This game features a total of more than $133 million in prizes, nine of them worth $1 million to $5 million, and overall odds of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Two top prizes of $5 million each and four $1 million prizes remain unclaimed. Johnson requested minimal publicity. The Pic N Pac #11 located at 969 Interstate 10 E. in Seguin sold the winning ticket, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $171 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $22 million.
