LEAGUE CITY WOMAN IS SET FOR LIFE
(AUSTIN) ­ Martha L. Smith of League City claimed a top prize in the Set For Life instant game from the Texas Lottery®. She'll receive $5,000 per week for 20 years, not to exceed $5,000,000. This is the second of three top prizes claimed in this version of the popular scratch-off game. Also sharing in her luck, the Star Tobacco Mart located at 1821 E. Main St. in League City is eligible to receive a retailer bonus of $10,000 for selling the winning ticket. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $54 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $9 million.
