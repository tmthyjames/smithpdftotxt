FOURTH $1 MILLION PRIZE CLAIMED IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER TEXAS LOTTERY® SCRATCH-OFF
(AUSTIN) ­ Houston resident George Chappel claimed the fourth of 40 prizes worth $1 million each in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game $500,000,000 Blockbuster. The instant game features more than half a billion in total prizes, and 10 top prizes of $2.5 million remain unclaimed. Overall odds of winning are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. For selling the winning ticket, the N Star Foods located at 10104 Tidwell Road in Houston is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is worth an estimated $94 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $19 million.
