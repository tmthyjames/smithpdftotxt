SAN ANTONIO RESIDENT WINS $1 MILLION WITH $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER SCRATCH-OFF TICKET
(AUSTIN) ­ Oscar Flores of San Antonio claimed a $1 million prize in the $500,000,000 Blockbuster game from the Texas Lottery®. The game features overall odds of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Eight top prizes of $2.5 million and 31 prizes of $1 million each remain unclaimed. Flores purchased his ticket at the Big Diamond #1023, 1002 Castroville Road in San Antonio. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible to receive a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's advertised jackpot for Texas Two Step® is $1.2 million. Wednesday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is worth an estimated $45 million.
