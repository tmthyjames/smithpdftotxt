TAKE 5 PRIZE OF $50,000 SURPRISES VIDOR RESIDENT
(AUSTIN) ­ When she purchased her Take 5 ticket, Dina Ewart didn't realize it would take her all the way to the Texas Lottery® Claim Center in Beaumont. Ewart and her husband were riding together when they stopped at the Country Pantry #10 located at 990 E. Railroad in Vidor. Inside, she bought a $5 Take 5 ticket to play in the vehicle. "I started scratching and realized there was a five," said Ewart, explaining how she uncovered the winning number on her ticket. "So, I looked to see what the prize was." Still, she didn't realize what she had right away. "I scratched it and saw the 5 and thought, `Oh, I won $5.' Then I saw the zero and thought it was fifty. Then I saw the $50,000." "I immediately started shaking and screaming," she said. "Then I called my mom and told her, and she didn't believe me." Ewart won the fourth of eight $50,000 prizes in the Take 5 game. Odds of winning any prize are one in 3.88, including break-even prizes. "Since its introduction in July, the Take 5 game has paid out four prizes of $50,000, nine prizes of $5,000 and 98 prizes worth $1,000 each," said Texas Lottery Commission Deputy Executive Director Gary Grief.
