FOOTBALL FAN FROM ODESSA WINS SEASON TICKETS IN FIRST DALLAS COWBOYS DRAWING
(AUSTIN) ­ Football fan Christy Ruple of Odessa claimed one of 20 pairs of Dallas Cowboys 2010 season tickets awarded in a recent secondchance drawing for the Dallas Cowboys instant game. One game in particular, though, has her really excited. Having moved to Texas from the Philadelphia area, she hopes to see the Cowboys play the Eagles. Ruple says, although she was very surprised to hear about her win, she has no qualms about the road trips to Dallas. "It's like five hours, but that's okay. I have a brand new car." The $5 Dallas Cowboys scratch-off ticket offers players a chance at cash prizes up to $100,000.
