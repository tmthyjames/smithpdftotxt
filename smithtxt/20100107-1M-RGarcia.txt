13 MAY BE LUCKY FOR $500,000,000

BLOCKBUSTER $1 MILLION WINNER
(Austin) ­Rosalinda Garcia of San Antonio was the lucky 13th winner of a $1 million secondtier prize in the $500,000,000 Blockbuster Texas Lottery® scratch-off game. There are 27 prizes still unclaimed at that level, and another seven prizes worth $2.5 million each remain unclaimed. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes.

Garcia purchased her ticket at the Fort Sam Chevron located at 2207 N. Pan Am Expressway in San Antonio. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000.

This claimant requested minimal publicity. Friday's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $48 million. Saturday's Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $31 million.

For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Web site at http://www.txlottery.org.
