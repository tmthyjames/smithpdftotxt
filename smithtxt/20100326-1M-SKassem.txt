SAN ANTONIO RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION PRIZE IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER GAME
(Austin) ­ Sami S Kassem of San Antonio claimed the 17th prize of $1 million in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game $500,000,000 Blockbuster. He purchased his winning ticket at the H-E-B Food Store #480 located at 9900 Wurzbach Road in San Antonio. The $500,000,000 Blockbuster broke the record for most prizes ever offered in a Texas Lottery scratch-off game, more than half a billion dollars in total. Another 23 prizes worth $1 million each and six top prizes of $2.5 million remain unclaimed. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Kassem isn't the only one who will benefit from this win. The store is also eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus for selling the winning ticket. This claimant requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $40 million. Saturday's drawings include an estimated Powerball® jackpot of $60 million and an estimated Lotto Texas® jackpot of $66 million.
