EIGHTEENTH PRIZE OF $1 MILLION AWARDED IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(Austin) ­ The $500,000,000 Blockbuster is living up to its reputation as a big game, awarding the eighteenth second-tier prize of $1 million. Usha Daftary of Plano claimed the prize at the Texas Lottery® Field Office in Dallas. With more than half a billion dollars in total prizes, the $500,000,000 Blockbuster features overall odds of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Six top prizes worth $2.5 million each and 22 second-tier prizes of $1 million remain unclaimed. The winning ticket was purchased at the Tom Thumb #2554 located at 3100 Independence Parkway in Plano, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $143 million. Wednesday's drawings include an estimated Powerball® jackpot of $252 million and an estimated Lotto Texas® jackpot of $80 million. ###
