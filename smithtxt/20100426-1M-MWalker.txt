$1,000,000 GOLD RUSH AWARDS $1 MILLION TOP PRIZE TO PLAYER FROM SOUTHEAST TEXAS
(Austin) ­ The rush is on in southeast Texas. The Texas Lottery® scratch-off game $1,000,000 Gold Rush awarded its first top prize of $1 million to Mary A. Walker of Highlands. Introduced in February, the $1,000,000 Gold Rush game features more than $44 million in total prizes and three top prizes of $1 million each. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.60, including break-even prizes. For selling the winning ticket, JR's Minutemaid located at 7724 Decker Drive in Baytown is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The claimant requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $191 million. Wednesday's drawings include an estimated Lotto Texas® jackpot of $84 million and an estimated Powerball® jackpot of $30 million.
