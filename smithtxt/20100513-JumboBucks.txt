NEW TEXAS LOTTERY® SCRATCH-OFF GAMES TO OFFER SECOND CHANCE TO WIN CASH PRIZES UP TO $1 MILLION
(Austin) ­ At one time or another, everyone needs a second chance, and that is exactly what the new Jumbo Bucks games offer. Monday, May 17, three new versions of the classic game will launch ­ Jumbo Bucks III, Giant Jumbo Bucks III and Mega Jumbo Bucks III ­ offering instantwin prizes up to $250,000 and second-chance prizes up to $1 million. "This is the first time we've offered a full suite of games at different price points with such large cash prizes available through second-chance drawings," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Gary Grief. "All three Jumbo Bucks games also feature impressive instant-win prizes and increased prize payouts." The $2 Jumbo Bucks III game features a total of more than $41 million in prizes, with instant-win top prizes of $50,000 and second-chance prizes worth up to $100,000. At $5, the Giant Jumbo Bucks III scratch-off has total prizes exceeding $72 million, including instant prizes up to $100,000 and second-chance prizes up to $500,000. With more than $90 million in total prizes and instant-win top prizes of $250,000, the $10 Mega Jumbo Bucks III will offer a chance to win up to $1 million in each of the four second-chance drawings. Only non-winning tickets can be entered into the second-chance drawings. The entry deadline for the first second-chance drawing will be July 30. Detailed information on how to enter the drawings is printed on the back of the tickets or is available on the Texas Lottery® website. For more information about these or other Texas Lottery games, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
