TEXAS LOTTERY® ANNOUNCES FREE JACKPOT GAME TICKET PROMOTION
(Austin) ­ Texas Lottery® players who enjoy jackpot games have reason to celebrate. Beginning June 6 and continuing until June 26, players who purchase $10 or more on a single Powerball® ticket will receive a free $1 Mega Millions® Quick Pick ticket and a free $1 Lotto Texas® Quick Pick ticket. Players can take advantage of the Multi-Draw and Power Play® options to reach the $10 purchase level. Multi-Draw allows players to choose the same numbers for up to 10 drawings, with each playboard costing $1 per drawing. Power Play allows players to increase their non-grand/jackpot prizes by the multiplier selected for an additional $1 per playboard. Tonight's Mega Millions jackpot is an estimated $17 million. Tomorrow's Powerball jackpot is an estimated $20 million, and the Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $5 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
