EDNA RESIDENT CLAIMS ONE OF THREE TOP PRIZES IN $1,000,000 GOLD RUSH SCRATCH-OFF GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ The second top prize worth $1 million has been claimed in the $1,000,000 Gold Rush game. Santiago Zarate of Edna claimed his prize at the Texas Lottery® claim center in Victoria and requested minimal publicity. Zarate's win is also good news for Smitty's Food Mart located at 9 FM 444 South in Inez. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. The $1,000,000 Gold Rush scratch-off game was introduced in February and features more than $44 million in total prizes. One top prize of $1 million remains unclaimed, and overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.60, including breakeven prizes. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $12 million. Saturday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $11 million.
