HOUSTON RESIDENT CLAIMS $500,000,000

BLOCKBUSTER PRIZE WORTH $1 MILLION
(AUSTIN) ­ Audencio Paz of Houston claimed a $500,000,000 Blockbuster prize worth $1 million at the Texas Lottery® Claim Center in Houston. Six top prizes of $2.5 million and 18 second-tier prizes of $1 million remain unclaimed. Overall odds of winning any $500,000,000 Blockbuster prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Paz, who requested minimal publicity, purchased the winning ticket at the E Z for U location at 2526 W. Mount Houston in Houston. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $12 million. Tomorrow's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $5 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official website at www.txlottery.org. ###
