23RD PRIZE OF $1 MILLION AWARDED IN $500,000,000 BLOCKBUSTER GAME
(AUSTIN) ­ Ricky Herring of Nacogdoches claimed the 23rd second-tier prize worth $1 million in the Texas Lottery® game $500,000,000 Blockbuster. There are now 17 prizes of $1 million each and six top prizes of $2.5 million remaining unclaimed in the popular scratch-off game. With more than half a billion dollars in total prizes, overall odds of winning any $500,000,000 Blockbuster prize are one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. The Western Hills Mini Mart, 4684 FM 343 in Nacogdoches, will also benefit from Herring's win. The store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus for selling the winning ticket. Herring requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $41 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $60 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $9 million.
