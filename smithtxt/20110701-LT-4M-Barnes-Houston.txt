HOUSTON RESIDENT CLAIMS $5 MILLION LOTTO TEXAS® JACKPOT AT LOTTERY HEADQUARTERS IN AUSTIN
(Austin) ­Reese Barnes of Houston today claimed a Lotto Texas® jackpot advertised at $5 million for the drawing on Wednesday, June 22. Barnes chose the Cash Value Option, which gives him a lump-sum prize of more than $3.3 million, before taxes. Barnes bought the Lotto Texas Quick Pick ticket at Skays Chevron, 10720 S. Post Oak, in Houston. The retailer is eligible to receive a bonus of $50,000 for selling this ticket. The numbers selected in the drawing were 50-23-48-10-16-24. In addition to Barnes' jackpot prize, 13 tickets matched five of six numbers, winning each ticket-holder $2,311. Barnes requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $105 million. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $6 million, and the Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $20 million. More information about the Texas Lottery is on the official website, www.txlottery.org.
