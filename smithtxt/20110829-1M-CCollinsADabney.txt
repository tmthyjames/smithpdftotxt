TWO SCRATCH-OFF PRIZES WORTH $1 MILLION EACH WON IN THE GREATER HOUSTON AREA
(Austin) ­ There are two new winners in the Houston area today. Clarence Collins of Pearland and Arthur Dabney of Houston claimed $1 million prizes today from two different $20 Texas Lottery® scratch-off games. Collins purchased his winning $500,000,000 Blockbuster ticket at Pearland Food, 4415 Broadway St., making the store eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000. The Park Place Travel Plaza located at 5168 Interstate 10 NE in Sealy is also eligible for a $10,000 bonus for selling Dabney his winning $500 Million Frenzy ticket. Each of the games features more than $500 million in total prizes and overall odds of winning any prize of one in 2.77, including break-even prizes. Two top prizes of $2.5 million each and eight $1 million prizes remain unclaimed in the $500,000,000 Blockbuster. The $500 Million Frenzy has nine prizes of $2.5 million and 31 second-tier prizes of $1 million each remaining unclaimed. Both claimants requested minimal publicity. Tomorrow's Mega Millions® jackpot is an estimated $21 million. Wednesday's Powerball® jackpot is an estimated $75 million, and the Lotto Texas® jackpot is an estimated $23 million.
