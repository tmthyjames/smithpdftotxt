GTECH NAMED APPARENT SUCCESSFUL PROPOSER FOR TEXAS LOTTERY COMMISSION OPERATOR CONTRACT
(Austin, August 7) - The GTECH Corporation today was named the Apparent Successful Proposer by the Texas Lottery Commission for the Lottery Operations and Services Request for Proposals (RFP). An "Apparent Successful Proposer" is the entity recommended by the evaluation committee and approved by the executive director, subject to the negotiation and execution of a completed contract. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
