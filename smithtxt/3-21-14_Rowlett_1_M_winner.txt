ROWLETT LOTTERY PLAYER WINS $1 MILLION 50X THE CASH TOP PRIZE
(AUSTIN) ­ Adam L. Gilbreath of Rowlett claimed $1 million in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game, 50X The Cash. The Bass Pro Beverages at 4400 Bass Pro Drive in Garland sold the winning ticket, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

Gilbreath's prize is the third of 10 top prizes to be claimed for this game. With more than $143 million in total prizes, the 50X The Cash game features overall odds of winning of one in 3.06, including break-even prizes. In addition to the 10 top prizes, millions of prizes from $10 to $50,000 currently remain unclaimed.

This claimant requested minimal publicity.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $42 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $16 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
