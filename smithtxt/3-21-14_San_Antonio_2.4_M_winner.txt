SAN ANTONIO TRUST WINS MONTHLY BONUS TOP PRIZE WORTH $2.4 MILLION
(AUSTIN) ­ The ABC Living Trust, Broadway National Bank, Trustee, by Debra Lynn Reichenbach, Vice President, Trustee, of San Antonio has claimed a Monthly Bonus top prize worth $10,000 per month for 20 years, totaling $2.4 million before taxes.

The winning ticket was purchased at the TETCO #316 located at 8928 Culebra Road, Suite 1, in San Antonio. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000.

This is the third of four Monthly Bonus top prizes to be claimed. With more than $52 million in total prizes, the Monthly Bonus game features odds of one in 3.94, including break-even prizes. In addition to the final top prize, eight second-tier prizes of $20,000 currently remain unclaimed.

This claimant requested minimal publicity.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $42 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $16 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
