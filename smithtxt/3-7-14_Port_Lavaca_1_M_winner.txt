PORT LAVACA LOTTERY PLAYER WINS $1 MILLION WITH $500,000,000 EXTREME CASH BLAST SCRATCHOFF GAME

(AUSTIN) ­ Patricia Ann Canady of Port Lavaca claimed a $1 million prize with the $500,000,000 Extreme Cash Blast scratch-off game from the Texas Lottery®. The ticket was purchased at First Convenience located at 1916 W. Austin in Port Lavaca. For selling the winning ticket, the retailer is eligible for a $10,000 bonus.

With more than $531.1 million in prizes, the $20 game features overall odds of winning any prize of one in 2.84, including break-even prizes. The game offers 50 cash prizes from $1 million to $2.5 million and more than 1,000,000 prizes from $100 to $500.

The claimant requested minimum publicity.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $42 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $16 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
