Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - July 20, 2001 Contact: Kristina Tirloni , 512-344-5144

HOUSTON AREA GROUP CLAIMS $13 MILLION LOTTO TEXAS JACKPOT
(Austin, July 20) - Today, the Texas Lottery Commission presented a ceremonial check in the amount of $7,541,024.40 to the CSF Partnership of Houston. The group won the $13 million Lotto Texas jackpot from the drawing held on Saturday, July 7th. The partners chose the Cash Value Option, which pays approximately half of the estimated jackpot amount. The jackpot-winning ticket was purchased at A&L Postal Services, located at 5170 Aldine Mail Route in Houston. The retailer is eligible for a bonus of one-percent - $130,000 - for selling the winning Lotto Texas ticket. The winning numbers drawn were: 32-31-20-34-35-05. Representatives of CSF Partnership have requested minimal publicity. As of today, there is still one $13 million Lotto Texas ticket unclaimed for the drawing held June 23rd. The Quick Pick ticket was purchased at Copperfield Liquor #6, located at 7540 FM 1960 East in Humble. The estimated jackpot for Saturday's Lotto Texas drawing is $12 million. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
