SAN ANTONIO SCRATCH-OFF PLAYER WINS $2.5 MILLION 100X THE CASH PRIZE

(AUSTIN) ­ Richard Vasquez of San Antonio claimed a top prize worth $2.5 million in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game 100X The Cash.

With more than $183 million in total prizes, 100X The Cash features overall odds of winning of one in 2.86, including break-even prizes. In addition to six top prizes of $2.5 million that have not yet been claimed, 10 second-tier prizes of $100,000 each currently remain unclaimed.

The Hildebrand Grocery at 1502 W. Hildebrand Ave. in San Antonio sold the winning ticket, making the store eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000.

The claimants requested minimal publicity.
