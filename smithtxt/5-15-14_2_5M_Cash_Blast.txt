COLLEGE STATION TRUST WINS $2.5 MILLION TOP PRIZE IN $500,000,000 EXTREME CASH BLAST

(AUSTIN) ­ Lucky Dawg Investment Trust, Jeffrey C. Harris, Trustee, of College Station, claimed a $500,000,000 Extreme Cash Blast ticket worth $2.5 million. The ticket was purchased at the Wellborn Grocery located at 14889 FM 2154 in College Station, making the store eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

With more than $513 million in total prizes, $500,000,000 Extreme Cash Blast features overall odds of winning of one in 2.84, including break-even prizes. Seven top prizes of $2.5 million and 25 second-tier prizes of $1 million each currently remain unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
