$500,000,000 EXTREME CASH BLAST PLAYER FROM ROUND ROCK CLAIMS $1 MILLION SECOND-TIER PRIZE

(AUSTIN) ­ John Daniel of Round Rock claimed a $1 million second-tier prize in the $500,000,000 Extreme Cash Blast game. The ticket was purchased at the Avenue Food Mart located at 1700 Grand Avenue Parkway, Suite 101, in Pflugerville. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 Texas Lottery® retailer bonus.

With more than $513 million in total prizes, $500,000,000 Extreme Cash Blast features overall odds of winning of one in 2.84, including break-even prizes. Seven top prizes of $2.5 million and 25 second-tier prizes of $1 million each currently remain unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
