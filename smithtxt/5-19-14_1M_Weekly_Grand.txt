RICHMOND RESIDENT WINS $1,000 PER WEEK FOR 20 YEARS
(AUSTIN) ­ Rhonda Pines, of Richmond, claimed a Weekly Grand prize worth $1,000 per week for 20 years (not to exceed $1,040,000). The ticket was purchased at the Richmond Meat Market located at 140 Collins Road, Suite 500, in Richmond. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 Texas Lottery® retailer bonus.

With more than $39 million in total prizes, Weekly Grand features overall odds of winning of one in 3.96, including break-even prizes. One top prize currently remains unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
