Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - May 25, 2001 Contact: Leticia Vasquez, 512/344-5208

PHARR PARTNERSHIP COLLECTS TEXAS MILLION PRIZE
(Austin, May 25) - The Rojas and Smith Partnership of Pharr today collected the top prize of $1 million in the Texas Lottery's Texas Million game. Adriana Garza and Josefina Smith Rojas are the general partners. The winning ticket was purchased at the RC Mini Mart, located at I Road and Chaparral in San Juan. The drawing was held on Friday, May 4, 2001. For selling a winning ticket, the retailer is eligible to receive a one-percent bonus of $10,000. The winning numbers were 3-17-22-18. The numbers were selected randomly. Prizes of $1,000,000 must be claimed at Lottery Headquarters in Austin, and are not official until validated through Lottery Security. Members of the partnership requested minimal publicity. The last Texas Million drawing was held Friday, May 11th. The jackpot for tonight's Texas Two Step drawing is $350,000. Tomorrow's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $6 million. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
