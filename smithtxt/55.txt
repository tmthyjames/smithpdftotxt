GRAND PRAIRIE LOTTO TEXAS WINNER COLLECTS $3.4 MILLION JACKPOT PRIZE
(Austin, May 11) - The Texas Lottery today presented Jack Yother with a check in the amount of $3, 430, 672. 50 for matching the six winning numbers in the Lotto Texas $25 million drawing on April 21, 2001. This is the second of the four winning tickets sold in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex area for this drawing to be claimed. The four tickets shared the jackpot prize. The winning Quick Pick ticket was purchased with a Cash Value Option, which gives the winner the net present value of the jackpot, approximately half of the advertised jackpot. For selling one of the four winning tickets, the Circle S Food Stop #2 located at 1702 S. Beltline in Grand Prairie is eligible to receive a one-percent bonus of the prize, $62,500. The winning numbers were 47-02-23-10-29-48. Mr. Yother requested minimal publicity. Saturday's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $18 million. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
