TEXAS LOTTERY® RETAILER IN LAKE DALLAS SELLS 20X CA$H TICKET WORTH $1 MILLION
(AUSTIN) ­ Richard J McQuone of Hickory Creek purchased a 20X Ca$h ticket and scratched his way to a $1 million prize. For selling the winning ticket, the Beer Barn located at 307 W. Hundley Drive in Lake Dallas is eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000.

With more than $76 million in total prizes, 20X Ca$h features overall odds of winning of one in 3.05, including break-even prizes. One top prize of $1 million and thousands of prizes from $20 to $10,000 currently remain unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
