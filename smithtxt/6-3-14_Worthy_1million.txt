AUSTIN AREA LOTTERY PLAYER WINS $1 MILLION SCRATCH-OFF PRIZE

(AUSTIN) ­ Hans Worthy, of Leander, claimed a top prize worth $1 million in the Texas Lottery® $1,000,000 Jackpot scratch-off game. The Big Diamond, located at 901 Whitestone in Cedar Park, sold the winning ticket, making the store eligible for a retailer bonus of $10,000.

With more than $90 million in cash prizes, the game features overall odds of winning of one in 2.88, including break-even prizes.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.

About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $43 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $16 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
