MCALLEN RETAILER SELLS $1 MILLION WINNING SCRATCH-OFF TICKET

(AUSTIN) ­ The Town & Country CS located at 921 N. 10th St. sold an Instant Millions scratch-off ticket worth $1 million. The store is now eligible to receive a $10,000 retailer bonus. McAllen resident Jose Silva Jr claimed the first of five top prizes.

Introduced May 27, the Instant Millions game features more than $62 million in total prizes. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 2.91, including break-even prizes.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
