SAN ANTONIO SCRATCH-OFF PLAYER CLAIMS $2.5 MILLION 100X THE CASH TOP PRIZE

(AUSTIN) ­ Adrian Black of San Antonio claimed the fourth of eight $2.5 million top prizes in the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game 100X The Cash. For selling the winning ticket, the Valero Corner Store #2026 at 9209 S. Zarzamora St. in San Antonio is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

This was the second 100X The Cash top prize sold in San Antonio. Richard Vasquez of San Antonio claimed a $2.5 million prize in April. Vasquez purchased his ticket at the Hildebrand Grocery, 152 W. Hildebrand Ave.

With more than $183 million in total prizes, 100X The Cash features overall odds of one in 2.86, including break-even prizes. In addition to the four unclaimed top prizes, seven prizes of $100,000 and 49 prizes of $20,000 currently remain unclaimed.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
