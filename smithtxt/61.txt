Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - April 2, 2001 Contact: Kristina Childress , 512-344-5144

MONTHLY MONEY ADDS WEIMAR RESIDENT TO GROWING LIST OF LUCKY WINNERS
(Austin, April 2) - Today, the Texas Lottery Commission presented the Teresa Guerrero Trust of Weimar the first installment of $2,500 for winning the top-prize for the instant ticket Monthly Money. The top-prize awarded for the Texas Lottery instant ticket is $2,500 a month for five years, which totals $150,000. The winning scratch-off ticket was purchased at Fishbeck's Texaco and BBQ, located at 8015 Eagle Street in Weimar. This makes the 7th Monthly Money winner since the instant ticket was introduced last year. There is one top-prize Monthly Money scratch-off ticket left within the game. A representative of the Teresa Guerrero Trust requested minimal publicity. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
