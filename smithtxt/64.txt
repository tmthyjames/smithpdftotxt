Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - March 15, 2001 Contact: Keith Elkins, 512-344-5210

AUSTIN LOTTO TEXAS WINNER CLAIMS RECORD $85 MILLION JACKPOT PRIZE
(Austin, March 15) - The Texas Lottery Commission today paid RBH CNC, Ltd., of Austin a Lotto Texas jackpot prize in the amount of $51,214,323.00. The winner chose the Cash Value Option on the record $85 million prize offered for the drawing on Wednesday, March 7th. Shopper's Mart, located at 10500 N. Lamar Boulevard in Austin, sold the winning ticket. The retailer is eligible to receive a onepercent bonus of the jackpot prize not to exceed $500,000. The jackpot numbers were 51-36-10-45-31-37. The winning ticket was one of five purchased and was a Quick Pick. The winner requested minimal publicity for today in anticipation of a news conference tomorrow to which the media are invited. A news conference will be held at 11:15 a.m. Friday at Lottery headquarters located at 611 E. 6th Street in Austin. Saturday's estimated Lotto Texas jackpot is $11 million. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
