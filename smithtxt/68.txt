Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - March 6, 2001 Contact: Leticia Vasquez, 512-344-5208

HOUSTON LOTTO TEXAS WINNER COLLECTS $23 MILLION JACKPOT PRIZE
(Austin, March 6) - The Texas Lottery today presented RDK Revocable Trust, Paul C. Looney, trustee, with a check in the amount of $12,421,990.00 for matching the six winning numbers in the Lotto Texas drawing on January 27th, 2001. The winning ticket was purchased with a Cash Value Option. The numbers were randomly selected. For selling the winning ticket, the Randall's store located at 12555 Westheimer in Houston is eligible to receive a one-percent bonus of $230,000. The winning numbers were 14-35-45-19-33-04. The winner requested minimal publicity. Wednesday's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $77 million. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
