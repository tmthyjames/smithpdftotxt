Pasadena Player Claims September $4 Million Lotto Texas® Jackpot
(Austin, October 13) - Deborah Patty of Pasadena claimed the jackpot matching Lotto Texas® ticket for the September 24 drawing worth $4 million. Because Patty chose the Cash Value Option when purchasing her ticket, she will receive a lump-sum payment of $2,475,649.60, before taxes. "It was a real pleasure meeting our newest Lotto Texas winner," said Robert Tirloni, products manager for the Texas Lottery Commission. "Last month was a real trial for our coastal residents, and to now get the opportunity to relay some happy news from that time is especially enjoyable." Patty purchased her ticket at Stop N Go, located at 3926 Country Road #A in Pasadena. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $40,000 for selling the jackpot ticket. The numbers for the September 24 drawing were: 7 - 10 - 16 - 22 - 33 and the Bonus Ball number was 42. The winner has requested minimum publicity.
