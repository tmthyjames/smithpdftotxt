SAN ANTONIO LOTTERY PLAYERS CLAIM $2 MILLION AND $1 MILLION PRIZES
(AUSTIN) ­ A $2 million Mega Millions® prize and a $1 million Instant Millions prize have been claimed. Both winning tickets were sold by San Antonio retailers.

The C&D General Partnership, Christine D. Rendon, Partner, of San Antonio, claimed a Mega Millions® ticket worth $2 million from the drawing held Tuesday, May 27. The Babcock Chevron at 6392 Babcock Road in San Antonio sold the winning ticket.

Joshua W. Simon of San Antonio claimed a $1 million prize from the Instant Millions scratch-off game. The ticket was purchased at the Broadway Grocery-SK Express located at 8342 Broadway in San Antonio. This was the second of the game's five top prizes of $1 million to be claimed.

Both claimants requested minimal publicity.
