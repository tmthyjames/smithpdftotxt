California Lottery Commission Votes To Join Mega MillionsTM
(Austin, February 8) - The Texas Lottery Commission was pleased to learn that the California Lottery Commission has voted to become the 12th member of the multi-state lottery game, Mega MillionsTM. The Texas Lottery Commission looks forward to successful contract negotiations related to California's inclusion in Mega Millions. During this process, Mega Millions will continue to operate as it has since Texas joined the game in 2003. The Texas Lottery® marked its first full year of participation in Mega Millions on December 3, 2004, with sales in excess of $234 million. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www.txlottery.org ...
