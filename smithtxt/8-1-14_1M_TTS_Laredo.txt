$1,000,020 TEXAS TWO STEP® PRIZE CLAIMED BY TEXAS LOTTERY® PLAYER FROM LAREDO

(AUSTIN) ­ Reynaldo Ramirez of Laredo claimed a Texas Two Step® prize worth $1,000,020. His ticket matched all of the numbers drawn for the drawing held July 24 (6 ­ 11 ­ 18 ­ 25, Bonus Ball 7).

Ramirez purchased his ticket at the Valero Corner Store #1015 located at 2215 S. Zapata Highway in Laredo. The store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus for selling the winning ticket.

Drawings for Texas Two Step are held Mondays and Thursdays at 10:12 p.m. Jackpots start at $200,000 and roll until someone wins. Overall odds of winning any prize are one in 32.4, with a $5 guaranteed prize for matching only the Bonus Ball.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
