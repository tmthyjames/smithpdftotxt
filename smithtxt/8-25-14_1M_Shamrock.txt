SHAMROCK RESIDENT CLAIMS $1 MILLION TEXAS LOTTERY® SCRATCH-OFF PRIZE

(AUSTIN) ­ Koleen Hensley, of Shamrock, claimed a $1 million top prize from the Texas Lottery® scratch-off game, One Million Cash. Hensley purchased the ticket at the Chugs located at 108 East 12th Street in Shamrock. For selling the winning ticket, the store is eligible for a $10,000 retailer bonus.

This is the first of four top prizes to be claimed for this game. One Million Cash offers more than $88.4 million in prizes. Overall odds of winning any prize in One Million Cash are one in 2.80 including break-even prizes.

The claimant requested minimal publicity. About the Texas Lottery Beginning with the first ticket sold in 1992, the Texas Lottery has generated $21 billion in revenue for the state and distributed $44 billion in prizes to lottery players. Since 1997, the Texas Lottery has contributed $16 billion to the Foundation School Fund, which supports public education in Texas. As authorized by the Texas Legislature, certain Texas Lottery revenues benefit state programs including the Fund for Veterans' Assistance.
