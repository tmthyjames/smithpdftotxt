VICTORIA PARTNERSHIP CLAIMS $36.25 MILLION LOTTO TEXAS® JACKPOT Winning ticket was purchased in Cuero

(AUSTIN) ­ Lucky Dog Future, LP, Jamie Schacherl, Vice President, of Victoria, claimed the Lotto Texas® jackpot from the drawing held May 31. For selling the winning ticket, the Stripes #9416, located at 111 E. Morgan in Cuero, received a $362,500 retailer bonus.

The partnership's ticket matched all six numbers drawn (3-5-9-32-36-37) for the advertised $36.25 million jackpot. The claimant selected the cash value option valued at $24,228,956.39.

Lotto Texas drawings are held every Wednesday and Saturday evening at 10:12 p.m.

The claimant requested minimal publicity.
