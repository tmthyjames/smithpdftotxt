***CALENDAR LISTING*** JOIN WILLIAM SHATNER & THE TEXAS LOTTERY® FOR GUINNESS WORLD RECORD ATTEMPT

WHO: WHAT:

William Shatner, the Texas Lottery Commission and Star Trek fans. To kick-off the new Star TrekTM scratch-off game, the Texas Lottery® will attempt to set a Guinness World Record by having the most people dressed in Star Trek costumes at one event! At least 1,041 attendees in costume are needed to break the record. In addition to live music provided by DJ Marshall Leak III, the event will include chances to win prizes including $50,000, a trip to Las Vegas to attend the Star Trek convention and Texas Lottery merchandise. The trip package includes airfare for two, hotel accommodations for five nights, dinner for two, car service and $1,000. Participants must be at least 18 years old and dressed as a Star Trek character to enter the drawings.

WHEN:

Saturday, March 30 10 a.m.-1 p.m. CST Dallas Convention Center 650 South Griffin Street Dallas, TX 75202 Free and open to the public For more information about the event, follow the Texas Lottery on Facebook and Twitter for updates. ###
