Texas Lottery Commission Announces Instant Ticket Manufacturing And Services Successful Proposers
(Austin, August 31) - The Texas Lottery Commission today announced that the Apparent Successful Primary Proposer for its Request for Proposals for Instant Ticket Manufacturing and Services is Scientific Games International Inc. Additionally, it was announced that the Apparent Successful Secondary Proposer is Pollard Banknote Ltd. Upon completion of successful contract negotiations, the primary vendor will be the prime source of Texas Lottery instant tickets and services, while the secondary vendor will print a minimum of one game per contract year for the Texas Lottery and will serve as a backup in the event the primary vendor is unable to perform.
