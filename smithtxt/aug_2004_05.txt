Second Chance Worth $17,000 For Snyder Instant MONOPOLYTM Player

Lisa Ponce of Snyder was the top-prize winner in the first Instant MONOPOLYTM Second Chance Drawing.

(Austin, August 26) - Lisa Ponce of Snyder had actually forgotten all about her entry for the Instant MONOPOLYTM Second Chance Drawing, so she was pleasantly surprised to find a message on her answering machine from the Texas Lottery®. "I was pretty happy when I got the message because I thought I'd won the MONOPOLYTM prize package," said Ponce. "But then I called in and found out I'd actually won $17,000 and I was really happy!" In addition to the cash, Ponce also won a Franklin Mint Collector's Edition MONOPOLY game board. Ponce was the biggest winner in the first of four Second Chance Drawings for the $2 Instant MONOPOLY scratch off game. "When Instant MONOPOLY debuted last month, it zipped right past Go and shot to the top of our best-selling scratch off games," said Reagan E. Greer, Executive Director of the Texas Lottery Commission. "The Second Chance Drawings double the fun and make it an ever better value for our players." Ponce plays lots of Texas Lottery scratch off games, but definitely ranks Instant MONOPOLY as her new favorite. She said she plans to pay off some bills and expects her two children to help her figure out what to do with the rest. "My kids were more excited than me and they immediately wanted to find out what their cut would be," explained Ponce. Instant MONOPOLY offers prizes in the following amounts: $25,000, $2,500, $1,000, $200, $100, $50, $25, $15, $10, $8, $5, $3 and $2.
