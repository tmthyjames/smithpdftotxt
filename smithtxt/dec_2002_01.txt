TxLottery: News Release

Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - December 30, 2002 Contact: Leticia Vasquez, 512-344-5208

Media Advisory re: 900 Phone Number
(Austin, December 30) - As of January 1, 2003, the 900 phone number for Texas Lottery drawing results will no longer be available. To obtain drawing results, please visit the Texas Lottery Commission's website, www.txlottery.org, where the information is posted immediately following a drawing. Results are also available at any retail outlet that sells Texas Lottery tickets. .... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes. For more information about the TLC, please visit the official website at http://www.txlottery.org
