Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - December 28, 2004 Contact: Kristina Tirloni, 512-344-5144

Lewisville Man Wins First Top Prize For Texas Lottery® Set For Life Scratch Off Game

Mansoor Bemani of Lewisville has won $5,000 a week (*not to exceed $5,000,000 total) in the scratch off game "Set for Life."

(Austin, December 28) - Mansoor Bemani of Lewisville today became the first Texan to claim the top cash prize for the SET FOR LIFE scratch off game from the Texas Lottery®. Bemani will collect $5,000 a week for 20 years, with the total payout for the instant ticket equaling up to $5 million. "Just coming off the holiday weekend, and Mr. Bemani makes the second sizeable prize winner to collect a little holiday cash from the Texas Lottery," said Reagan E. Greer, executive director of the Texas Lottery Commission. "Yesterday, we had a topprize winner for $1 million, a $175,000 Mega Millions winner and a $14,312 Lotto Texas winner from different parts of the state. It's been a wonderful way to end the 2004 year." Bemani purchased his ticket at Park Chevron at 1925 Dallas Parkway in Plano. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the grand prize-winning ticket. Players still have the chance to win one more $5,000-a-week prize in the SET FOR LIFE scratch game. Other cash prizes in the game include; $2,500, $1,000, $500, $200, $100, $50, $20 and $10. The cost of the ticket is $10. Mansoor Bemani has requested minimum publicity.
