Mansfield Winner First To Collect Top-Prize From Lottery's First $30 Scratch Game
(Austin, December 23) - Today, the Texas Lottery® welcomed the first top-prize winner for the Holiday Millions Wishes scratch-off game. Bear Creek Custom Homes, L.L.C., of Mansfield, claimed the $2 million prize. The ticket was purchased at the Classic Minute Market located at 4098 I-20 East in Willowpark. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $10,000 for selling the top-prize winning ticket. "Holiday Millions Wishes is the first $30 instant ticket the Texas Lottery has ever released," said Executive Director Reagan E. Greer. "It has been tremendously popular among our players, accounting for more than $31 million in sales since its release in October. In fact, last week's instant ticket sales overall were nearly $1.9 million, or 3.5 percent, higher than the previous week, and $11.9 million, or 26.9 percent, higher than the same week in 2003." As of today, there are still three more $2 million grand prizes unclaimed. Holiday Million Wishes also has cash prizes of $20,000, $2,000, $500, $300, $100, $70, $40 and $30. The winner has requested minimum publicity. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www. txlottery.org
