TxLottery: News Release

Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - February 13, 2002 Contact: Kristina Tirloni, 512.344.5144,

Media Advisory - Fort Worth: Riverside Food Mart Check Presentation
(Austin, February 13) - - The Texas Lottery Commission will be presenting a ceremonial retailer bonus check in the Fort Worth area to Riverside Food Mart. The lottery retailer sold the jackpot winning Lotto Texas ticket for the drawing held on December 26, 2001. The jackpot, worth an estimated $36 million, was won by a group of coworkers from a local Fort Worth pharmacy. In accordance with lottery rules, the retailer received a bonus of one percent of the jackpot amount - $360,000.
Who: What: Linda Cloud, Executive Director, Texas Lottery Commission Retailer Check Presentation

When: Friday, February 15, 2002, 1:30 p.m. Where: Riverside Food Mart 350 N. Riverside Drive Fort Worth, Texas

On hand for the ceremony will be storeowner Sankar Saha. The Triple J NXS Trust claimed their Lotto jackpot on January 16th. Their Quick Pick ticket was purchased with the 25-year annuity payment option. The trust received an initial payment of $1,460,149.20, and will receive payments of $1,440,000 every December for the next 24 years. -30.... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes. For more information about the TLC, please visit the official website at http://www.txlottery.org
