Farm To Market Road Lined with Lucky Lottery Retailers
(Austin, February 8) - - The Humble/Atascocita area must rest beneath a lucky star. Since 1994, there have been five Texas Lottery retailers on the same farm to market road, FM 1960 East, that have sold jackpot winning Lotto Texas tickets.

The location of the five stores along a three-mile stretch of FM 1960 that sold $125 million worth of winning tickets.

The most recent six-of-six matching ticket was sold for the Lotto drawing held Saturday, February 2, 2002. There were two tickets matching all six numbers for the estimated $10 million jackpot, and one of those tickets was sold at the Albertson's located at 7506 FM 1960 East. The retailer is eligible for a bonus of $50,000 for selling one of the jackpot winning tickets. Today, Max Shannon of Atascocita turned that winning Lotto ticket over to the Texas Lottery. Shannon purchased his winning ticket with the 25-year annuity option. He received an initial payment of $200,000, and will receive the same amount every February for the next 24 years. The winner has requested minimum publicity. The numbers drawn were: 02-10-21-29-30-18. Last year, for the Lotto drawing held on June 23rd, there was a single jackpot winning ticket sold at Copperfield Liquor #6 at 7540 FM 1960 East. The jackpot was worth an estimated $13 million. The jackpot was never claimed before the 180-day deadline. In accordance with lottery rules, the unclaimed prize, $7,302,952.80, will be transferred to the State's Multicategorical Teaching Hospital Account. In 1998, the Exxon Company store located at 7435 FM 1960 East, sold a sole winning ticket sold for the Lotto drawing held on July 25th. The winner, Funkel, Ltd., of Kingwood purchased the ticket with the Cash Value Option and received $3,823,803.20. For the drawing held on July 31, 1996, there were three jackpot winning Lotto tickets sold. One of the three was sold at Atascocita Liquors at 5311 FM 1960 East. The retailer received a bonus of $56,143.37 for selling one of the jackpot-winning tickets.
