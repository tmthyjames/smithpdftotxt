Texas Lottery Commission Names Greer New Executive Director

Reagan Greer, the new Executive Director of the Texas Lottery Commission.

(Austin, February 28) - Today, the three-member Texas Lottery Commission named Reagan Greer as the new Executive Director of the agency. Greer most recently served as Bexar County District Clerk and brings government and corporate management experience to the position. He has held numerous leadership roles, recently serving as a member of the Texas Electronic Government Task Force and the Texas Online Authority. He chaired the Texas Uniform Electronic Transaction Act (UETA) Task Force and was a liaison with the Texas Judicial Council on Information Technology. Greer was recognized in 2002 by the North San Antonio Chamber of Commerce as the Nonprofit Technology Leader of the Year, and in 2001 the Texas Association of Counties Leadership Foundation acknowledged him as Chairman of Bexar County Committee for Innovative and Efficient Use of Government in a community. Bexar County Commissioners Court awarded him with an Hidalgo Service Award in January 2003 for his work as District Clerk. Prior to serving as District Clerk, Greer served as a Justice of the Peace and spent two decades in the real estate industry in sales, marketing and management. He also served on the Board of Directors of the San Antonio and Texas Association of Realtors and chaired numerous committees of each. Greer, who brings government management experience and an outside business perspective to the Texas Lottery Commission, begins immediately at an annual salary of $110,000.
