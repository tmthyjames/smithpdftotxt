Lotto TexasTM - Highest Since July 2003 And Highest In North America Today
(Austin, January 13) - Tomorrow night's Lotto TexasTM jackpot is worth an estimated $48 million. What does that mean for Texans and other Lotto Texas players in bordering states? It means they're vying for the largest lottery jackpot in North America. "We're bringing players higher jackpots and more ways to win with Lotto Texas, and we're pleased that with this roll, we are the number one jackpot in North America," said Reagan E. Greer, Executive Director of the Texas Lottery Commission. "This is also the highest Lotto jackpot since July of 2003." As of today, the top 10 jackpots around North America are:
