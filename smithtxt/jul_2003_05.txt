Lotto TexasTM Jackpot Biggest In Nation For Wednesday's Drawing
(Austin, July 28) - That old saying, "Everything"s big in Texas," has a new twist today: Texas has the largest lottery jackpot in the country for Wednesday"s drawing. The jackpot for the Texas Lottery"s flagship game, Lotto TexasTM, is an estimated $47 million, a new high for the recently revamped game. "We said the changes to Lotto Texas would bring higher jackpots, and we"re pleased that with this roll, we are the number one jackpot in the country," said Reagan E. Greer, Executive Director of the Texas Lottery Commission. "We also said the new game with its eight prize levels and 1-in-57 odds of winning some prize would generate more winners," Greer noted. "The winners for Saturday"s drawing are a new high. While we didn"t have a winner for the $41 million jackpot, we did have 138,433 other winners and a total of $951,384 in prizes for that drawing."
