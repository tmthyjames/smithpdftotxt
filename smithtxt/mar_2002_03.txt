Austin Man Cashes In On Weekly Grand
(Austin, March 27) - - The Texas Lottery Commission today paid Jose Cuevas Lizardo of Austin the first installment of the top prize in the Weekly Grand instant game. After receiving today's installment of $52,000, the winner will receive 19 additional annual payments in the amount of $52,000. The total prize comes to $1,040,000. Diamond Shamrock #1310 located at 1900 S. Mays in Round Rock sold the winning ticket. The retailer is eligible to receive a one-percent bonus of $10,000 for selling the prize-winning ticket. This is the 54th Weekly Grand winner since the game began in 1995. Weekly Grand has been one of the most popular Texas Lottery instant tickets and has been reissued a total of six times. The winner requested minimal publicity. Tonight's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $16 million. -30.... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
