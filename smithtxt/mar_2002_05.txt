Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - March 22, 2002 Contact: Kristina Tirloni, 512-344-5208,

Houston Area $13 Million Lotto Texas Jackpot Claimed
(Austin, March 22) - The Texas Lottery today paid Texzona Investments, Ltd., the first installment of the $13 million Lotto Texas jackpot it won for the drawing on March 2. The managing partner is Penny Elkins. The first annuity share paid today was in the amount of $520,000. The remaining 24 payments in the same amount will be made on March 15 each year. Handi Stop #19, 9721 Jones Road in Houston sold the winning ticket. The retailer is eligible to receive a one-percent bonus of $130,000 for selling the jackpot-winning ticket. The claimant selected the winning numbers, 51--04--52--27--17--25. Saturday's Lotto Texas jackpot is an estimated $13 million. -30.... The Texas Lottery, the 4th largest selling lottery in North America, is "Focusing on the Future of Education, One Ticket At A Time." By providing the 'Games of Texas' for more than 10 years, the Texas Lottery Commission (TLC) has generated more than $10 billion for the state and paid nearly $17 billion to players in prizes.
