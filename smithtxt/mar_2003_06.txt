Keller Resident Today Claims Lotto Texas Jackpot Prize From Texas Lottery Commission
(Austin, March 17) - Today, Tina Hanks of Keller claimed her Lotto TexasTM jackpot prize from the drawing held Saturday, December 14, 2002. The jackpot was worth an estimated $4 million. Hanks chose the Cash Value Option, which gives her a total payout of $2,407,440, before taxes. The Quick Pick ticket was purchased at Albertson's #4193, located at 8428 Denton Highway in Watauga. The retailer received a bonus of 1 percent of the jackpot amount--$40,000--for selling the jackpot-winning ticket. The numbers drawn were: 40-21-27-03-17-31. The winner requested minimum publicity. As of today, there is one Lotto Texas jackpot still unclaimed. There was one ticket sold in San Antonio for the drawing held on Wednesday, January 29, worth an estimated $5 million. Wednesday's Lotto Texas jackpot is worth an estimated $35 million.
