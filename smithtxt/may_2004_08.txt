Round Rock Partnership Collects $175,000 Prize Won In May 7 Mega Millions Drawing
(Austin, May 17) - The Right Five, a partnership from Round Rock, today collected a prize of $175,000 won in the Mega MillionsTM drawing on May 7. Dennis Hoban, a partner in the partnership, claimed the prize for the Right Five. The winning Quick Pick ticket was purchased at the 7-Eleven #1662-26815 located at 1713 North Mays in Round Rock. "It's a lot of fun when a group wins," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Reagan E. Greer. "After taxes, the partnership's prize is $131,250, and when it's divided among the 11 members of the group, it comes close to $12,000 for each one. That's pretty good walking-around money." The members of the partnership requested minimum publicity. Fans of Mega Millions can expect a jackpot of an estimated $19 million on the boards for Tuesday night's drawing. Lotto Texas® aficionados will play for a jackpot worth and estimated $57 million on Wednesday night.
