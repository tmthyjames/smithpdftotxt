Austin Texas Lottery® Winner Claims Cash FiveTM Is "Lucky" Game
(Austin, October 10) - Roberto Enamorado says the Texas Lottery® Cash FiveTM game has been very lucky for him. He explained that he plays everyday and his Quick Pick numbers finally came up Monday night. "I'm always telling my friends and family to play," Enamorado said. "You never know when your luck will change." Enamorado collected the top cash prize for the Cash Five drawing held on Monday, October 6. His prize came to $48,932, before taxes. He purchased his ticket at Stop N Go #2141, located at 9433 Parkfield Drive in Austin. The retail owner is eligible to receive a bonus of $489.32 for selling the five-of-five matching ticket. "So far this week, players matched numbers to win more than $847,000 in the Texas Lottery's Cash Five game," said Reagan E. Greer, Executive Director of the Texas Lottery Commission. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www. txlottery.org
