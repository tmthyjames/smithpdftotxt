Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - October 14, 2004 Contact: Media Relations, 512-344-5131

Wednesday's Lotto TexasTM Drawing Occurs After Technical Difficulties Resolved
(Austin, October 14) - Technical difficulties with a drawing machine prevented the live broadcast of last night's Lotto TexasTM drawing. The drawing was conducted after the 10:12 p.m. live broadcast, in accordance with pre-established Texas Lottery Commission drawings procedures, and the numbers selected were certified by the independent auditor, Davila, Buschhorn and Associates, P. C. Those numbers were 10-11-30-42-44 and the Bonus Ball number was 4. "This was the first time in nearly 12 years of Lotto Texas drawings that we've had this sort of incident and I want to ensure all Texas Lottery players that the security and integrity of our games remain intact," said Reagan E. Greer, Executive Director of the Texas Lottery Commission. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www. txlottery.org. ... The Texas Lottery supports public education by contributing to the Foundation School Fund.
