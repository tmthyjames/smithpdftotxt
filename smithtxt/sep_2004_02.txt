Texas Lottery Commission Press Release - September 27, 2004 Contact: Robert Elrod, 512-344-5436 Media Relations, 512-344-5131

New Scratch Off Game Offers Players Chance To Be Set For Life
(Austin, September 27) - Set For Life, the new $10 scratch off ticket from the Texas Lottery®, offers players a chance to win $5,000 a week (not to exceed $5 million total)! The game also offers more than 257,000 prizes of $50 to $500. Each ticket offers players 10 chances to win. "Set For Life is just the latest entry into our exciting lineup of scratch off games," said Texas Lottery Commission Executive Director Reagan E. Greer. "With great prizes and great odds, I expect Set For Life to be another big hit with our players. And, as always, Texas education will be the biggest winner, with all net proceeds going to the state's Foundation School Fund." The odds of winning any prize are one in 3.49, including break-even prizes. In addition to the two grand prizes, Set For Life also offers prizes in the following amounts: $2,500, $1,000, $500, $200, $100, $50, $20 and $10. For more information about the Texas Lottery, please visit the official Texas Lottery Commission Web site at http://www. txlottery.org ... The Texas Lottery supports public education by contributing to the Foundation School Fund.
